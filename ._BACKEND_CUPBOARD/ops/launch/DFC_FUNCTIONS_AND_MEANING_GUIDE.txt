DFC FUNCTIONS AND MEANING GUIDE
Owner voice: DFC Founder (DataFight Central)
Positioning: Humanity-first combat sports platform

1) WHAT DFC MEANS
Data Fight Central is a unified support system for combat sports.
It connects preparation, performance, recovery, opportunity, and legacy.

Core principle:
- AI is support, not authority.
- Data is context, not obsession.
- People are the priority.

2) WHO DFC SERVES
- Fighters: visibility, readiness, opportunity
- Gyms and coaches: athlete development + discovery
- Promoters/fight shows: event reach + trusted signals
- Fans/community: meaningful updates and local discovery

3) CORE APP FUNCTIONS (AND WHY THEY MATTER)
A) Authentication + Roles
- Function: secure onboarding with role-aware experience
- Meaning: people are seen for who they are in the ecosystem

B) Command Center / Dashboard
- Function: daily readiness snapshot, performance signals, guidance
- Meaning: helps athletes make safer, smarter daily decisions

C) FightWire / Social Signals
- Function: live combat updates, opportunities, event and community feed
- Meaning: creates fairer access to information and opportunity

D) Training + Wellness
- Function: track effort, recovery, stress, and consistency
- Meaning: protects long-term athlete health and career longevity

E) Discovery (Gyms/Events/People)
- Function: local and regional discovery tools
- Meaning: strengthens real community links, not just online noise

F) Promotion + Marketplace Direction
- Function: pathways for promoters, gyms, and partnerships
- Meaning: supports sustainable growth across the full combat economy

G) Analytics + AI Coach Support
- Function: trend explanations and practical recommendations
- Meaning: guidance without shame, pressure, or unsafe claims
