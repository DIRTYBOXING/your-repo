PILOT LICENCE CHECKLIST — DATA FIGHT CENTRAL
=============================================
Give this checklist to your lawyer to convert into a short pilot licence
template you can sign quickly.

PARTIES AND DEFINITIONS
- Promoter: [Name, ABN/ACN]
- Distributor: Data Fight Central (D.F.C) [ABN]
- Event definition: [Event name, date, venue]
- Content: live broadcast feed, VOD highlights, fighter imagery, logos

GRANT OF RIGHTS
- [ ] Live transmission rights
- [ ] VOD highlights rights (30-day window)
- [ ] Exclusivity scope (if any — recommend non-exclusive for pilot)
- [ ] Sublicensing rights (if applicable)

TERRITORY
- [ ] Explicit country list OR "worldwide"
- [ ] Geoblocking obligations per territory

PLATFORMS
- [ ] Web (datafightcentral.web.app)
- [ ] iOS (future)
- [ ] Android (future)
- [ ] Connected TV (future)

TERM AND WINDOWS
- [ ] Live date and time
- [ ] VOD window length (e.g., 30 days post-event)
- [ ] Replay rights duration

COMMERCIAL TERMS
- [ ] Revenue split: Promoter 60% / DFC 40% (or negotiated)
- [ ] Alternative: Guaranteed fee + revenue share
- [ ] Fees and deductions (Stripe processing ~2.9% + 30c)
- [ ] Reserve for chargebacks (7-14 days)
- [ ] Settlement schedule (net 30)

PAYMENT FLOW
- [ ] Promoter to onboard Stripe Connect (Express account)
- [ ] DFC routes promoter share automatically via transfer_data
- [ ] DFC takes commission via application_fee only
- [ ] DFC does NOT hold promoter funds
- [ ] Dispute and refund handling process
- [ ] Audit rights — promoter can request transaction logs

TECHNICAL DELIVERY
- [ ] Feed format (SDI/RTMP/HLS) and delivery endpoint
- [ ] Delivery deadline (minimum 48-72 hours before event for testing)
- [ ] Metadata and graphics timing
- [ ] Encoder redundancy requirements

PROTECTION REQUIREMENTS
- [ ] DRM: FairPlay (iOS/Safari) / Widevine (Android/Chrome) / PlayReady (Edge)
- [ ] Server-side forensic watermarking
- [ ] Geoblocking per territory specified in licence
- [ ] Takedown cooperation and response time obligations

REPORTING AND AUDIT
- [ ] Daily/weekly view and revenue reports
- [ ] Watermark logs provided post-event
- [ ] Audit rights and access to logs (upon reasonable request)
- [ ] Post-event report delivered within 7 days

ANCILLARY CLEARANCES
- [ ] Promoter warrants they hold fighter releases
- [ ] Music clearances obtained
- [ ] Sponsor permissions confirmed
- [ ] No third-party claims on content

INDEMNITIES AND LIABILITY
- [ ] Mutual indemnities for IP infringement
- [ ] Liability caps (recommend proportional to deal value)
- [ ] Insurance requirements (if applicable)

TERMINATION
- [ ] Breach remedies (cure period: 14 days)
- [ ] Takedown obligations on termination (14 business days)
- [ ] Effect on settlement — outstanding payments still owed
- [ ] Surviving clauses (indemnity, confidentiality)

CONFIDENTIALITY AND PUBLICITY
- [ ] Permitted announcements and marketing rights
- [ ] Use of promoter's name/logo in DFC marketing (with approval)
- [ ] Non-disclosure of commercial terms

GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
- [ ] Jurisdiction: Queensland, Australia
- [ ] Dispute resolution: mediation first, then arbitration
- [ ] Legal costs allocation

SIGNATURES
- [ ] Promoter authorised signatory
- [ ] DFC authorised signatory (DFC Founder, Founder)
- [ ] Date of execution
- [ ] Witness (recommended)
