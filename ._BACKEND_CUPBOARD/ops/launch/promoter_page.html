What we require before public PPV sales

  • Signed one-page Pilot Licence granting DFC the right to stream and sell the Event.
  • Witnessed Statutory Declaration or Affidavit from an authorised promoter representative confirming rights and attaching supporting documents.
  • Stripe Connect Express onboarding so promoter receives payouts directly; DFC does not hold promoter funds.
  • Technical test pass for DRM and forensic watermarking.

Download the templates, get the Stat Dec witnessed by a JP or solicitor in Queensland, upload the scanned copy, then book a tech call.

How to sign and upload

  1. Download the Pilot Licence and Statutory Declaration.
  2. Get the Statutory Declaration witnessed by a JP or solicitor in Queensland; scan and upload.
  3. Complete Stripe Connect onboarding (Express).
  4. Book a tech call and schedule the 72-hour test window.
  5. We run DRM + watermark tests; once passed and licence is signed, you may go public.
