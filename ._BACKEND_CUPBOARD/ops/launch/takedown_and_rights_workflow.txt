TAKEDOWN, RIGHTS VERIFICATION, AND OPERATIONAL WORKFLOW
========================================================
Data Fight Central (DFC) — Internal and publishable reference.

TAKEDOWN WORKFLOW (PUBLIC)
---------------------------
Step 1: Report takedown to legal@datafightcentral.com with:
        - Timestamp of infringing content
        - URL or stream reference
        - Evidence of rights ownership (contracts, releases)

Step 2: DFC suspends the stream or VOD pending verification within
        4 hours of receipt during event windows, 24 hours otherwise.

Step 3: DFC requests proof of rights from the content provider
        (statutory declaration, licence, fighter releases).

Step 4: If the provider cannot produce proof within 48 hours, DFC
        removes the content permanently and initiates refund/settlement
        per the Pilot Licence terms.

Step 5: If the claim is disputed, DFC follows the dispute resolution
        clause in the Pilot Licence and preserves watermark logs for
        forensic tracing. Both parties are notified of the dispute
        process timeline.

RIGHTS VERIFICATION CHECKLIST (INTERNAL — BEFORE EVERY EVENT)
---------------------------------------------------------------
- [ ] Witnessed Statutory Declaration present and scanned
- [ ] Pilot Licence signed by both parties and stored
- [ ] Fighter releases attached, signed, and dated
- [ ] Sponsor clearances attached
- [ ] Music licence confirmations attached
- [ ] Stripe Connected Account ID recorded
- [ ] DRM test evidence stored (screenshots, licence issuance logs)
- [ ] Watermark test evidence stored (extraction verification)
- [ ] Watermark logs retained for minimum 12 months
- [ ] All documents saved to ops/audit/[event-name]/

PRE-SALE GATE (MUST ALL BE TRUE BEFORE PUBLIC PPV SALE)
---------------------------------------------------------
1. Pilot Licence signed: YES / NO
2. Statutory Declaration witnessed and received: YES / NO
3. Stripe Connect account active and verified: YES / NO
4. DRM test passed (FairPlay + Widevine): YES / NO
5. Watermark test passed and extraction verified: YES / NO
6. Technical test passed (clean feed, no dropped frames, 30 min): YES / NO
7. All fighter releases on file: YES / NO

If ANY item is NO, do not open public PPV sales.

EVENT AUDIT LOG (CSV FORMAT — ONE ROW PER EVENT)
--------------------------------------------------
Fields:
Event, Promoter, Stripe_Connected_ID, Licence_Signed, StatDec_Received,
DRM_Test_Date, Watermark_Test_Date, Tech_Test_Date, Live_Date,
Gross_Buys, Gross_Revenue, Net_To_Promoter, DFC_Commission, Notes

Save as: ops/audit/event_audit_log.csv

FORENSIC WATERMARK RETENTION
------------------------------
- All watermark extraction logs retained for 12 months minimum.
- Logs include: viewer ID, session ID, timestamp, extraction result.
- Used for: piracy tracing, takedown evidence, legal proceedings.
- Storage: encrypted, access restricted to DFC legal and ops team.

ANTI-PIRACY MONITORING (DURING AND POST-EVENT)
-------------------------------------------------
- Active monitoring during live event window.
- Automated scanning for restreams on major platforms (YouTube, Twitch,
  Facebook Live) during event.
- DMCA/takedown notices sent within 1 hour of detection.
- Post-event monitoring for 30 days (VOD window).
- Monthly summary report to promoter.
