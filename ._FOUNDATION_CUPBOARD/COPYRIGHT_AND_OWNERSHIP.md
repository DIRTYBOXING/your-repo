## INVESTOR/PARTNER PROTECTIONS

### If You Take Investment

Protect yourself with:

1. **Vesting Schedule**
   - Your equity vests over 4 years
   - 1-year cliff (you keep nothing if you leave in year 1)
   - Protects you from being pushed out

2. **Founder Control**
   - Maintain majority voting rights
   - Board seat guaranteed
   - Veto power on major decisions

3. **IP Assignment**
   - Investors get equity, NOT intellectual property
   - You retain moral rights as creator
   - Clear that you built this

### Red Flags to Avoid:

❌ Any agreement where you "assign all rights" without compensation  
❌ Non-compete clauses that prevent you from working in tech  
❌ Unlimited liability for platform issues  
❌ Giving away >50% equity in first funding round

## LEGAL SHIELD: LIABILITY PROTECTION

### 1. Form a Legal Entity (PRIORITY)

**Recommended:** Limited Liability Company (LLC) or Corporation

**Benefits:**

- Personal assets protected from business debts
- Professional credibility
- Tax advantages
- Easier to raise funding

**Cost:** $100-$500 to form (DIY online services like LegalZoom)

**Steps:**

1. Choose business name: "Data Fight Central LLC" or "DIRTYBOXING LLC"
2. File with your state (online, takes 15 minutes)
3. Get EIN (tax ID) from IRS (free, instant online)
4. Open business bank account
5. Keep business and personal finances SEPARATE

### 2. Platform Disclaimers (Already in Docs)

Include on every page:

```
Data Fight Central provides information and communication services
for combat sports professionals. We are not responsible for:

- Accuracy of user-provided information
- Outcomes of fights or training
- Financial transactions between users
- Injuries sustained during training or competition
- Third-party services or events

Use of this platform is at your own risk. See full Terms of Service
and Disclaimers at [link].
```

### 3. Insurance (When Revenue Allows)

**Priority Order:**

1. **General Liability Insurance** ($300-500/year)
   - Protects against injury/property damage claims
   - Get when you have 100+ active users

2. **Professional Liability (E&O)** ($500-1000/year)
   - Protects against errors/negligence claims
   - Get when processing payments

3. **Cyber Liability** ($1000-2000/year)
   - Protects against data breaches
   - Get when storing sensitive user data

### Your Obligations

You must implement reasonable measures to:

1. **Verify User Identity** (when possible)
   - Email verification required
   - Phone verification for paid accounts
   - Photo ID for professional fighters (optional)
