**1. AI Safety Coach (Not Just Performance)**

## 7. Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**Risk:** Cloud costs exceed projections  
**Mitigation:** Firebase Blaze plan with budget alerts; optimize database queries; implement caching

**Risk:** AI Coach provides harmful advice  
**Mitigation:** Strict prompt engineering; human review of flagged conversations; crisis keyword triggers

**Risk:** Data breach or privacy violation  
**Mitigation:** Firebase App Check; end-to-end encryption; regular security audits; GDPR compliance

### Community Risks

**Risk:** Platform used for exploitation or harassment  
**Mitigation:** AI-powered content moderation; community reporting system; verified mentor background checks

**Risk:** Suicide or self-harm discussed on platform  
**Mitigation:** Real-time keyword monitoring; automatic crisis line pop-ups; trained moderators on-call

**Risk:** Gambling or match-fixing infiltration  
**Mitigation:** Zero tolerance policy in Terms of Service; IP blocking of known gambling domains; community reporting
