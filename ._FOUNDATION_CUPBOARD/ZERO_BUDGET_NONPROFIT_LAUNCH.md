## 📍 STEP 1: CHOOSE YOUR NONPROFIT STRUCTURE

### Option A: 🇦🇺 **Australian Nonprofit** (Recommended if you're in Australia)

**Entity Type:** Incorporated Association (cheapest option)

**Cost:** $37 - $135 (one-time, varies by state)

- Victoria: $135.40
- NSW: $87
- Queensland: $37.60

**Registration Process:**

1. Choose a name: **"Data Fight Central Foundation Inc."**
2. Draft constitution (free template from Consumer Affairs Victoria)
3. Get 5 founding members (can be friends/family)
4. File incorporation papers with Consumer Affairs (your state)
5. Apply for ABN (free) at abr.gov.au
6. Apply for DGR (Deductible Gift Recipient) status via ATO (optional, but helps with donations)

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks
**Authority:** Consumer Affairs Victoria / Your State's Fair Trading Office
**Link:** [consumer.vic.gov.au/clubs-and-fundraising](https://www.consumer.vic.gov.au/clubs-and-fundraising/incorporated-associations)

---

### Option B: 🇳🇿 **New Zealand Nonprofit** (Cheaper option if you're in NZ)

**Entity Type:** Incorporated Society

**Cost:** $50 NZD (one-time)

**Registration Process:**

1. Choose a name: **"Data Fight Central Foundation Society"**
2. Draft constitution (free template from Societies Office)
3. Get 15 founding members (can be online friends willing to sign)
4. File incorporation with Companies Office NZ
5. Apply for Charities Registration (free) with Charities Services
6. Get IRD number (free)

**Timeline:** 4-6 weeks
**Authority:** Companies Office NZ + Charities Services
**Link:** [societies.govt.nz](https://www.societies.govt.nz/)

### Option C: 🌍 **Fiscal Sponsorship** (ZERO COST, Fastest)

**What It Is:** An existing charity "sponsors" your project. They handle the legal/tax stuff, you focus on building.

**Cost:** $0 upfront. They take **5-15%** of donations (only when you raise money)

**Best Fiscal Sponsors for Tech/Social Good:**

1. **Fractured Atlas** (US-based, global projects) — fracturedatlas.org
2. **Open Collective Foundation** — opencollective.com/foundation
3. **The Hack Foundation** (for developers doing social good) — hackfoundation.org
4. **GitHub Sponsors** (if you're building open source tech) — github.com/sponsors

**Process:**

1. Apply to fiscal sponsor (free)
2. They approve your project
3. You use THEIR nonprofit status for Google Grants
4. They handle all legal/tax paperwork
5. You focus 100% on building DFC

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks
**Google Grants:** You apply using the fiscal sponsor's credentials

**RECOMMENDATION:** Start with fiscal sponsorship NOW, then register your own nonprofit later once you have funding.

## 🚀 STEP 3: APPLY FOR GOOGLE FOR NONPROFITS (FREE)

### Process:

1. **Go to:** [google.com/nonprofits](https://www.google.com/nonprofits)

2. **Click "Get Started"**

3. **Enter Organization Details:**

   - Country: Australia / New Zealand
   - ABN / Charities Registration Number
   - Website: datafightcentral.web.app

4. **Verify Nonprofit Status:**
   - Connect TechSoup account (or fiscal sponsor's verification)
   - Upload incorporation certificate

5. **Complete Eligibility Survey:**
   - Mission: Mental health, youth support, community safety
   - Beneficiaries: Combat athletes, at-risk youth, DV survivors
   - Programs: Crisis intervention, AI coaching, community network

6. **Submit Application**

**Timeline:** 2-7 days for approval  
**Cost:** $0
