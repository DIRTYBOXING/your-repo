## 🎨 Design Principles Applied

### Color Psychology:

- **Warm oranges/ambers** = sunrise, energy, hope, warmth (NOT darkness)
- **Gold** = value, opportunity, uplift
- **Soft pinks** = compassion, healing, care
- **Greens** = life, growth, renewal

### Visual Metaphors:

- ☕ **Coffee** = morning ritual, energy, fresh starts
- 🌅 **Sunrise** = new beginnings, hope dawning
- 💪 **Strength symbols** = transformation, empowerment
- 🤝 **Community** = together we rise

### Language Energy:

- **High-energy verbs**: Give, Create, Spark, Fuel, Transform, Thrive
- **Life-affirming nouns**: Hope, Life, Energy, Sunrise, Fresh Starts
- **Emotional resonance**: Not guilt/pity, but EMPOWERMENT and SOLIDARITY
