## 💰 REVENUE SPLIT & PAYMENT TERMS

### **Sliding Revenue Agreement**

**DFC Platform Fee: 30–50%** of gross revenue (sliding scale based on event exposure)  
**Promoter Receives: 70–50%** of gross revenue (higher DFC share as buy count grows)

> DFC uses a **linear sliding scale** — not fixed tiers. At low volumes the platform
> takes 30%; as total purchases approach 10,000+ the DFC share rises to 50%.
> This reflects DFC's increasing infrastructure, bandwidth, and promotional cost
> at scale.

### **What "Gross Revenue" Means**

- All PPV purchases (full event, main event, single fight)
- All merchandise sales tied to your event
- All sponsorship revenue processed through platform
- **BEFORE** payment processor fees (Stripe/PayPal ~2.9% + $0.30)

### **Payment Processing Fee Responsibility**

- Payment processor fees (Stripe: 2.9% + $0.30, PayPal: 3.49% + fixed fee) are deducted FIRST
- Sliding split applies to NET amount after processor fees
- **Example Calculation (low volume — 30% DFC floor):**

  ```
  Customer pays: $25.00 (full event)
  Stripe fee: -$1.03 (2.9% + $0.30)
  Net amount: $23.97

  Promoter gets: $16.78 (70%)
  DFC gets: $7.19 (30%)
  ```

- **Example Calculation (high volume — 50% DFC ceiling):**

  ```
  Customer pays: $25.00 (full event)
  Stripe fee: -$1.03 (2.9% + $0.30)
  Net amount: $23.97

  Promoter gets: $11.99 (50%)
  DFC gets: $11.99 (50%)
  ```

### **Payment Schedule**

- **Weekly payouts:** Every Monday for previous week's sales (Mon-Sun)
- **Minimum payout:** $50 threshold (rolls over to next week if under)
- **Payment method:** Bank transfer, PayPal, or Wire (Partner choice)
- **Currency:** USD, AUD, EUR, GBP (Partner chooses default)

### **Transparent Reporting**

- Real-time dashboard shows every purchase, timestamp, amount
- Export CSV/PDF reports anytime
- Monthly summary emailed automatically
- No hidden fees, no surprise deductions

### **Arbitration (Final Step)**

1. **Binding arbitration:** If mediation fails, arbitration is FINAL (no court appeals)
2. **Rules:** JAMS Comprehensive Arbitration Rules & Procedures
3. **Arbitrator:** One arbitrator, mutually selected (or JAMS appoints)
4. **Location:** Sydney, Australia (or video conference if both agree)
5. **Costs:** Losing party pays winner's arbitration costs + attorney fees
6. **Award:** Arbitrator decision binding, enforceable in any court
