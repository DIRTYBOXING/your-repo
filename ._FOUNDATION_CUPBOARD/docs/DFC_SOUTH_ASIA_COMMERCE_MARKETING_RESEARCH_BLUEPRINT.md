# DFC South Asia Commerce Marketing Research Blueprint

Status: operating blueprint for India and Pakistan market expansion.

Purpose: define how DFC turns India and Pakistan into revenue-producing markets through commerce, marketing, research, and distribution.

## Position

India and Pakistan are not side markets.

They are scale markets.

If DFC gets South Asia right, it gains:

- massive mobile-first combat audiences
- lower-friction creator-led growth loops
- strong WhatsApp and short-form sharing behavior
- local-language promotion leverage
- better pricing elasticity for PPV, replay, merch, and referral commerce

## Market Thesis

### India

- commerce shape: volume market
- buyer path: short-form clip -> WhatsApp share -> event page -> regional price -> PPV or replay
- strongest levers: Hindi and English copy, creator collaborations, gym referral packs, Sony LIV and JioCinema adjacency, FanCode-style framing

### Pakistan

- commerce shape: trust and community market
- buyer path: community referral -> WhatsApp and Instagram -> local language social proof -> event page -> partner route or DFC PPV
- strongest levers: Urdu and English copy, boxing and wrestling crossover framing, gym nodes, Tapmad and PTV Sports adjacency, diaspora bridge

## Four-Layer Blueprint

### 1. Commerce Layer

- regional price ladder for INR and PKR
- event page with clear local price intent
- replay-first offers for lower-ticket conversion
- creator and gym referral links by market
- local payment and trust messaging, even before full local payment rails exist

### 2. Marketing Layer

- creator packs for India and Pakistan separately
- gym packs with city-level targeting
- WhatsApp-forward creative variants
- Reels and Shorts cuts with Hindi, Urdu, and English caption variants
- region-specific launch calendars timed to local evenings

### 3. Research Layer

- city target list
- combat discipline demand by city
- creator inventory by market
- gym partner inventory by market
- pricing sensitivity and replay preference by market
- local broadcaster and platform adjacency map

### 4. Distribution Layer

- DFC owned route
- regional broadcast route
- creator distribution route
- gym node route
- diaspora route for UK, UAE, and North America spillover

## South Asia Operator Sequence

1. choose event and commercial offer
2. localize headline, CTA, and replay language for India and Pakistan
3. assign regional pricing targets
4. generate creator, gym, and sponsor packs for both markets
5. route to regional platforms and social channels
6. monitor clicks, buys, replay uptake, and referral conversion
7. rebalance creative and price ladders weekly

- entry replay offer
- standard live PPV

### Price posture
