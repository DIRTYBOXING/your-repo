## The Crisis

### Australia

- **1 in 6 children** (nearly **950,000 kids**) live in poverty
- **19,400 children** were homeless on a single census night
- **60,112 children** presented to homelessness services with families in 2022-23
- **22%** of children begin school developmentally vulnerable due to disadvantage

### New Zealand

- **1 in 7 children** (14.3%) — roughly **208,000 kids** — live in material hardship
- **156,600 children** regularly go without essentials like food, clothing, shoes, and heating
- Many children attend school hungry, without breakfast or proper nutrition

---

## What We Do

The **Gold Coin Drive** provides **direct, immediate support** to underprivileged children across Australia and New Zealand:

### ✅ Essential Needs

- **Food & Nutrition**: Breakfast programs, lunch supplies, nutritious snacks
- **Clothing & Shoes**: School uniforms, winter clothes, sports gear
- **School Supplies**: Books, stationery, backpacks, technology access

### ✅ Stability & Support

- **Housing Assistance**: Emergency accommodation support for families
- **Transport**: Bus passes, travel support for school attendance
- **Health**: Doctor visits, dental care, mental health support

### ✅ Opportunity & Growth

- **Sports Programs**: Free martial arts training, coaching, gym access
- **Mentorship**: Positive role models from the combat sports community
- **Life Skills**: Discipline, respect, confidence, resilience

### 1. **Donate**

## Why Combat Sports?

The **DFC Foundation** connects combat sports communities with vulnerable children because:

### 💪 Discipline Creates Stability

Martial arts training provides structure, routine, and purpose for children in unstable homes.

### 🛡️ Gyms Become Safe Havens

Boxing clubs, MMA gyms, and martial arts schools offer safe spaces away from violence and hardship.

### 🥊 Fighters Give Back

Coaches, fighters, and promoters understand struggle and resilience — they become powerful mentors.

### ⚖️ Everyone Deserves a Fair Fight

No child should start life at a disadvantage. The Gold Coin Drive levels the playing field.
