# Data Fight Central — Project Summary for Google Grants

## Mission

Data Fight Central empowers athletes, coaches, and fans to track, improve, and celebrate their health, fitness, and mental wellness. Our mission is to make elite sports science, AI coaching, and community support accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or background.

## Features & Impact

- Holographic dashboard for training, recovery, and performance
- Fight Camp diary/log for mental and physical health tracking
- Fempower page: dedicated to women fighters, inclusivity, and empowerment
- Real-time AI coach, analytics, and feedback
- Social sharing, community engagement, and support
- Accessibility-first design: high contrast, screen reader support, large fonts
- Inclusive: supports all genders, abilities, and backgrounds
- Community-driven: user stories, testimonials, and peer support

## Technical Readiness

- Built with Flutter (cross-platform: Android, iOS, Web)
- Firebase/Firestore backend for secure, scalable data
- Modular architecture: feature modules for easy updates
- CI/CD pipeline for automated builds and deployment
- Google Play Store ready: meets all guidelines, privacy, and accessibility standards
- Demo links, screenshots, and user flows available

## Innovation

- AI-powered training, recovery, and mental wellness insights
- Holographic UI for futuristic, engaging experience
- Fitness engines: track 15 fitness components, energy systems, and subjective ratings
- Fempower: unique platform for women fighters and supporters
- Social collaboration: share achievements, connect, and grow

## Need & Impact

- **Created by a survivor**: Retired fighter and disabled pensioner who battled mental illness, poverty, substance abuse, and survived the broken system. Built from lived experience—not theory.
- Addresses gaps in accessibility, inclusivity, and mental health in sports
- Funds and expands nonprofit community protection work, including NightChill
- Adds multiple protection layers for women in domestic violence situations (Guardian Mode hidden in period tracker)
- Supports vulnerable communities with poverty and recovery pathways
- Substance recovery support for fighters dealing with painkiller dependency
- Free crisis resources: domestic violence hotlines, mental health support, suicide prevention
- Potential reach: global sports and wellness community
