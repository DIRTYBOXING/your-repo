# DataFightCentral — Legal & Privacy Policy (Comprehensive)

_Last Updated: January 2025_

## 1. Overview

DataFightCentral ("DFC", "we", "us", "our") is a combat sports social media and training platform. This policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information, and outlines your legal rights.

**Mission Statement:** DFC is a pro-safety, anti-violence, anti-degradation platform. We exist to empower fighters, gyms, mentors, and communities through ethical technology.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Data:** Name, email, date of birth, profile photo, fight record, gym affiliations.
- **Content:** Posts, comments, media uploads, fight clips, training logs.
- **Communications:** Messages, support tickets, feedback.
- **Payment Data:** Billing address, payment method (processed by Stripe/third-party — we never store full card numbers).
- **Safety Data:** Emergency contacts, panic alert history (if enabled).

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Data:** Device type, OS version, app version, browser type.
- **Usage Data:** Feature interactions, screen views, session duration.
- **Location Data:** Approximate location for gym finder (only with explicit consent).
- **Performance Data:** Crash logs, error reports, analytics events.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Social Sign-In:** Google, Apple, or Facebook profile data (if you choose SSO).
- **Health Integrations:** Google Fit, Apple Health, Fitbit (only with explicit consent per integration).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose                     | Legal Basis (GDPR)   |
| --------------------------- | -------------------- |
| Provide core app features   | Contract performance |
| Safety & content moderation | Legitimate interest  |
| Analytics & improvement     | Legitimate interest  |
| Marketing communications    | Consent              |
| Legal compliance            | Legal obligation     |
| Emergency/panic alerts      | Vital interest       |

### 3.1 We Will NEVER:

- Sell your personal data to third parties.
- Use your data for discriminatory profiling.
- Share your location with other users without explicit consent.
- Use AI to make decisions that materially affect you without human review.

## 4. Pink Shield & Safety Commitments

DFC operates a **Pink Shield** certification program for victim-safe gyms and mentors. Safety data collected under this program is:

- Encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Accessible only to certified safety reviewers and admins.
- Never shared with gym owners, coaches, or other users.
- Subject to immediate deletion upon user request.

**Panic Alert Data:** If you trigger a panic alert, your approximate location and timestamp are shared only with your pre-set emergency contacts and DFC safety admins. This data is retained for 30 days maximum, then auto-deleted.

## 5. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

- **Service Providers:** Firebase (hosting/auth), Stripe (payments), analytics providers — all bound by DPAs.
- **Law Enforcement:** Only when legally required by valid court order or subpoena.
- **Emergency Services:** Only during active panic alerts, with user consent pre-granted.

We do NOT share data with advertisers, data brokers, or any entity for profiling purposes.

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 All Users

- **Access:** Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Fix inaccurate personal information.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Withdraw Consent:** Opt out of marketing, location tracking, or health integrations at any time.

### 6.2 EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

- Right to restriction of processing.
- Right to object to processing based on legitimate interest.
- Right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.
- Data Protection Officer contact: dpo@datafightcentral.com

### 6.3 California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

- Right to know what data we collect and why.
- Right to delete personal information.
- Right to opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell, but the opt-out is available).
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

### 6.4 Children & Minors

- DFC requires users to be **13+ years old** (COPPA compliance).
- Users aged 13-15 in the EU require parental consent (GDPR).
- Users under 16 are restricted from: messaging, marketplace, livestream features.
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If discovered, it is deleted immediately.

## 7. Data Security

- All data in transit encrypted via **TLS 1.3**.
- All data at rest encrypted via **AES-256** (Firebase/GCP defaults).
- Admin access requires **MFA** (multi-factor authentication).
- Role-based access control (RBAC) limits data access to authorized personnel.
- Regular security audits and penetration testing (quarterly).
- Incident response plan with 72-hour breach notification (GDPR requirement).

## 8. Data Retention

| Data Type        | Retention Period                        |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Account data     | Until deletion requested + 30-day grace |
| Posts/content    | Until deleted by user or moderation     |
| Analytics        | 26 months (anonymized after 13 months)  |
| Panic alerts     | 30 days                                 |
| Audit logs       | 7 years (legal requirement)             |
| Payment records  | 7 years (tax/legal requirement)         |
| Deleted accounts | Hard-deleted after 30-day grace period  |

## 9. Cookies & Tracking (Web)

DFC web uses only:

- **Essential cookies:** Authentication session, CSRF protection.
- **Analytics cookies:** Firebase Analytics (anonymized, no cross-site tracking).
- No third-party advertising cookies. No fingerprinting. No cross-site tracking.

## 10. AI & Automated Processing

DFC uses AI for:

- Content moderation (flagging harmful text/images).
- Fight performance analytics (from user-submitted data only).
- Personalized training recommendations.

**Safeguards:**

- No fully automated decisions with legal or significant effects.
- Users can request human review of any AI-driven moderation action.
- AI models are audited for bias quarterly.

## 11. International Data Transfers

Data is processed on Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) with servers in the US and EU. For EU users, transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and Google's Data Processing Addendum.
