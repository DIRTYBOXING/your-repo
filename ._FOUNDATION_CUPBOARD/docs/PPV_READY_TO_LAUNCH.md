## 🚨 COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: What if promoter says no to flat-fee?**  
A: Offer revenue-share instead (sliding 30–50% DFC model). Zero risk for them.

**Q: How do I get video files from promoters?**  
A: Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. Ask for highest quality (1080p+).

**Q: What if someone shares the Vimeo link?**  
A: Vimeo privacy settings prevent embedding. If it becomes an issue, upgrade to Dacast ($39/mo) with DRM.

**Q: How fast can I get paid from Stripe?**  
A: 2 days for first payouts, then can enable daily after 3 months + $10K volume.

**Q: Do I need an LLC?**  
A: Not immediately. Form it when you hit $5K revenue for liability protection.

**Q: Can I automate this?**  
A: Yes! After 10 events, hire a VA ($5/hr) to handle video processing, uploads, and customer support.

**Q: What if Fite.TV undercuts me?**  
A: They can't - they have higher overhead. Focus on value (community, features) not just price.
