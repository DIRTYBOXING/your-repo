# DFC Social Impact Program

## Purpose
Turn DFC platform reach into measurable social impact focused on preventing violence against women and children, supporting recovery pathways, and creating mentorship and opportunity through gyms and trainers.

## Campaign portfolio

### Pink Shields
Mission: prevention, survivor support, and safe-space rebuilds for women and children.

### Buy a Coffee Not a Coffin
Mission: one-click micro-donations funding emergency support, trauma services, and immediate relief.

### Gold Coin Gym
Mission: pay-what-you-can classes, mentor placements, and youth scholarships delivered through partner gyms and trainers.

## Program governance
- Program owner: DFC Social Impact lead
- Local partners: shelters, child welfare NGOs, community health services, councils
- Operational partners: gyms, trainer networks, mental health providers, legal aid partners
- Funding partners: sponsors, foundations, payment rails, local business matching partners

## Program components

### 1) Prevention and awareness
- Gym and community workshops
- School and youth outreach with local partners
- Short-form educational media and campaign explainers

### 2) Immediate relief and rebuild
- Emergency grants and practical support for survivors
- Safe-room or family-space rebuild support through vetted partners
- Legal and medical referral funding pathways

### 3) Mentorship and skills
- Trainer-led mentorship blocks
- Life-skills and vocational pathways
- Youth scholarships linked to Gold Coin Gym commitments

### 4) Sustainable income pathways
- Paid internship pathways through promoters and venues
- Local micro-business support where relevant
- Optional PPV or ticket revenue-share allocations to approved impact programs

### 5) Measurement and reporting
- Monthly internal dashboard updates
- Quarterly public impact reporting
- Annual audited impact and financial summary

## Fundraising and revenue channels
- In-app and web micro-donations at one-click levels
- Round-up contribution flow on eligible purchases
- Monthly supporter subscriptions with transparent impact benefits
- Event and PPV revenue-share allocations
- Co-branded campaign merchandise and limited drops
- Corporate matching, sponsor tiers, and partner badges

## Safeguarding and legal controls
- Background checks for all trainers and mentors working with minors
- Child protection policy and mandatory reporting process
- Minimal-PII handling with clear consent and encrypted storage patterns
- Insurance and liability controls for events, classes, and mentorship sessions
- Informed consent standards for testimonials and public stories

## Ethical boundaries
- Never expose survivor identity without explicit, informed consent
- Never use trauma narratives as marketing collateral without fair process and safety review
- Prioritize safety and dignity over growth metrics

## Launch plan

### 0 to 2 weeks
- Confirm program structure and legal routing
- Launch donation UX in app and web
- Sign partner MOUs with 2 to 3 NGOs and 3 to 5 gyms

### 2 to 6 weeks
- Launch one-city pilot with all three campaigns
- Publish campaign landing pages and donation receipts
- Release short campaign explainer media

### 6 to 12 weeks
- Expand to additional cities
- Onboard more trainers and partner gyms
- Run first PPV or event-linked revenue-share pilot
- Publish first public impact report

## KPI framework

### Fundraising
- Daily donation count
- Average donation size
- Donation conversion rate by surface
- Sponsor matching volume and retention

### Program operations
- Survivors supported
- Emergency grants disbursed
- Safe spaces rebuilt
- Mentorship hours delivered
- Youth placements in training or jobs

### Impact
- Immediate support outcomes (shelter, legal, medical support)
- Medium-term progression (training, employment, education)
- Community safety and retention indicators where trackable

## Ownership model
- Program lead: strategy, partners, accountability
- Operations lead: classes, disbursement, local coordination
- Product lead: donation UX, badges, reporting views
- Marketing lead: campaign creative and outreach cadence
- Legal and compliance lead: policy, MOU, insurance, safeguarding
