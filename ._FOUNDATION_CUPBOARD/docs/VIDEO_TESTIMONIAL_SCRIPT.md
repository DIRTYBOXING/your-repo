# DataFightCentral - User Testimonial Video Script

**Duration:** 1-2 minutes | **Format:** On-camera interview  
**Purpose:** Grant application, social proof, emotional resonance

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Format:**

- Single camera (webcam OK, smartphone OK, professional camera preferred)
- Quiet room with good natural lighting
- Neutral background (plain wall, gym, home office)
- Audio: Lavalier mic or smartphone microphone (clear)

**Framing:**

- Rule of thirds: subject's eyes in upper third of frame
- Shoulders visible, centered in frame
- Slight head tilt toward camera (warm, approachable)

**Tone:** Authentic, personal, directly from the heart

## INTERVIEW TIPS FOR AUTHENTICITY

**Before Recording:**

1. Let subject warm up (chat for 5 min before rolling)
2. Remind them: "Just be yourself. This isn't a commercial."
3. Ask them to speak slowly (tendency to rush in front of camera)
4. Have tissues available (emotional moments are powerful)

**During Recording:**

1. Ask follow-up questions to get deeper answers
2. "Tell me more about that" usually yields better sound bites
3. Capture B-roll if possible (subject training, meditating, on phone, etc.)
4. Record a few takes if possible

**After Recording:**

1. Get written consent to use their image/voice
2. Offer to obscure their identity if they prefer anonymity
3. Send them the final video before publishing

## LEGAL/ETHICAL NOTES

- **Consent:** Get written permission before using any personal story
- **Privacy:** Offer anonymity option (use first name + last initial, or alias)
- **Accuracy:** Don't exaggerate claims ("Cured my depression" ❌ vs "Helped me feel less alone" ✓)
- **Sensitivity:** If subject mentions suicide, include crisis resources in description
