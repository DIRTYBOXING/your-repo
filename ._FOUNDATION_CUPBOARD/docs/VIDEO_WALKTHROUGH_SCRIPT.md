# DataFightCentral - Product Walkthrough Video Script

**Duration:** 3-4 minutes | **Audience:** Grant reviewers, potential users, media  
**Production Date:** March 2026

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover OR presenter + screen  
**Quality:** 1080p minimum, shot at 24fps or higher  
**Audio:** Clear voiceover, background music (royalty-free, uplifting)  
**Branding:** Start/end with DataFightCentral logo (3 seconds each)

## TECHNICAL SPECS

| Element       | Requirement                                             |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Resolution    | 1080p (1920×1080) minimum                               |
| Frame Rate    | 24fps or 30fps                                          |
| Aspect Ratio  | 16:9                                                    |
| Audio         | 48 kHz, 16-bit stereo minimum                           |
| Length        | 3:20-3:50                                               |
| Music License | Royalty-free (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or similar)      |
| Voiceover     | Professional quality microphone (USB condenser minimum) |

---

## MUSIC RECOMMENDATIONS

- **Opening/Closing:** Cinematic, uplifting (0:00-0:05, 3:20-3:35)
- **Main content:** Steady, inspiring, motivational (0:20-3:15)
- **Intensity:** Moderate—background, not distracting
- **Genre:** Electronic, orchestral, or hybrid
- **Royalty-free sources:**
  - Epidemic Sound (epilepsy-friendly options)
  - Artlist
  - YouTube Audio Library
  - Incompetech
