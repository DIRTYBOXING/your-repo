# DataFightCentral — Complete Vision & System Definition

## 1. What DataFightCentral Is

DataFightCentral is a combat sports operating system.

It is simultaneously:

- A social platform
- A health & performance hub
- A sports analytics engine
- A promotion & advertising marketplace
- A discovery and matchmaking system
- An AI-assisted education platform

It is **NOT**:

- A betting app
- A medical service
- A gambling product

Everything is **educational, analytical, promotional, and consent-based**.

## 2. Core User Groups

- **Fighters**
- **Coaches & Mentors**
- **Gym Owners**
- **Promoters & Matchmakers**
- **Sponsors & Brands**
- **Fans & Media**
- **Admin / Moderation**

Each role has scoped permissions, tailored dashboards, and role-specific monetization paths.

## 3. Platform Pillars

### A. Social Combat Network

- Facebook-style feed (posts, likes, comments)
- Fighter branding pages
- Gym pages
- Event pages
- News aggregation
- Moderated, anti-bullying environment
- Reporting, blocking, and safety tools

### B. Health & Performance Hub

- **Powered by Google Fit (with consent)**
- **Tracked (non-medical):** Sleep, Weight, Heart rate, Stress, Hydration, Training intensity, Mood & motivation logs
- **Displayed as:** Neon dark charts, Line graphs, Pie charts, Stock-style trend visuals
- **AI Insights:** Training load trends, Recovery suggestions, Camp readiness indicators
- _⚠️ Always marked Educational Only_

### C. Fight Analytics & Rankings

- Fighter records & history
- Style breakdowns
- AI-assisted matchup analysis
- Rankings (ELO-inspired, transparent)
- “Fight Stocks” (engagement + performance index)
- _Clearly labeled non-financial. No gambling. No real-world trading._

### D. AI Systems (Google AI Studio Ready)

- **Uses:** Educational fight analysis, Style comparisons, Training pattern insights, Matchmaking suggestions, Content summarization.
- _No black-box decisions. No medical claims. No gambling advice._

### E. Promotion, Advertising & Marketplace

- Gym promotions, Fighter branding, Event promotion
- Sponsorship listings, Job board (camera, lighting, staff)
- Carousel ads, Map-based promotions, Discount codes
- **Analytics for advertisers:** Tracked via impressions & engagement.

### F. Maps & Discovery (Google Maps)

- Nearby gyms, Events, Accredited mentor gyms
- Promotions by location
- Fighters open to bouts
- Filter by discipline (Boxing, MMA, Muay Thai, Kickboxing)

### G. Streaming & PPV (Phase 8)

- Link-based embeds, Affiliate partnerships
- Rights-holder compliant

## 4. Data Collection (Transparent & Legal)
