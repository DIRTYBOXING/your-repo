# DFC Trainer Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
Standardize onboarding for trainers and mentors participating in Gold Coin Gym and related DFC social impact activities.

## Trainer profile
- Full legal name
- Contact details
- Gym or organization affiliation
- Role type: trainer, mentor, assistant coach, volunteer

## Required checks
- Identity verification complete
- Background check complete and acceptable
- Safeguarding or child-protection briefing complete
- Conflict of interest declaration complete

## Agreements
- Signed trainer participation agreement
- Signed safeguarding and conduct acknowledgement
- Signed privacy and confidentiality acknowledgement
- Signed media consent or media refusal declaration

## Training completion
- DFC campaign orientation complete
- Referral and escalation process training complete
- Session template and reporting process reviewed
- Incident response procedure reviewed

## Session readiness
- Assigned local coordinator
- Approved schedule and session format
- Beneficiary matching channel confirmed with partner NGO
- Emergency contact and escalation chain documented

## Program delivery expectations
- Minimum monthly mentor hours committed
- Attendance and session outcomes recorded
- Safety incidents reported immediately
- No direct sharing of sensitive beneficiary information outside approved channels

## Quality and review cadence
- Monthly check-in with local coordinator
- Quarterly performance and safeguarding review
- Refresher training when policy updates occur
