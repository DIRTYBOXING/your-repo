════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. PURPOSE & SPIRIT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DFC exists to promote promoters — not compete with them. This
partnership is built on collaboration, mutual respect, and a
shared mission to grow combat sports for fighters, fans, and
communities worldwide.

DFC operates as a platform and distribution channel for combat
sports content. Ultimate Legends retains full ownership and
creative control of their events, brand, and fighter relationships.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2. WHAT DFC PROVIDES (FREE OF CHARGE)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DFC will provide the following to Ultimate Legends at NO COST:

(a) EVENT LISTING & PROMOTION
    - Featured event listing on DFC platform (web + mobile)
    - Event details: fight card, venue, date, ticket links
    - Auto-generated typographic event poster (DFC original work)
    - Social feed promotion to DFC's combat sports audience
    - Cross-posting to connected social channels (Facebook, Instagram)
    - Push notifications to DFC users in the Melbourne/VIC region

(b) FIGHTER PROFILES & STATS
    - Fighter profiles for all Ultimate Legends card athletes
    - Fight history, records, and statistics tracking
    - AI-powered fight previews and analysis content

(c) SOCIAL AMPLIFICATION
    - DFC feed posts promoting upcoming Ultimate Legends events
    - Post-event results, highlights, and recaps
    - Fighter spotlight features and community engagement

(d) TECHNOLOGY PLATFORM
    - Fan engagement tools (live chat, predictions, scoring)
    - Audience analytics and event reach reporting
    - QR-code event passes (fighter, corner, media, VIP)

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3. OPTIONAL PREMIUM SERVICES (REVENUE SHARE)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The following services are available if Ultimate Legends chooses
to activate them. Each operates on a revenue share model — DFC
takes a percentage ONLY when money is made:

(a) PPV / LIVESTREAM via DFC
    If Ultimate Legends streams an event through DFC's Mux-powered
    streaming infrastructure:

    Revenue Split:
    - Ultimate Legends receives: 70-85% of net PPV revenue
    - DFC platform fee: 15-30% of net PPV revenue
      (sliding scale: DFC fee starts at 30% at 0 buys, decreases
       to 15% as buy count increases — the more you sell, the
       more you keep)
    - Stripe processing fees deducted before split (approx 2.9% + 30c)

    Pricing Tiers Available:
    - Full Show: $49.99 AUD
    - Main Card: $29.99 AUD
    - Prelims: $14.99 AUD
    - Single Fight: $9.99 AUD
    - Early Bird (limited time): configurable discount
    - VIP (backstage + multi-cam + moderated chat): premium pricing

    Replay/VOD: Included automatically. Replay access for 30 days
    post-event (configurable).

    DRM Protection: Widevine + FairPlay + PlayReady. Forensic
    watermarking on all streams. Device binding (max 2 concurrent).

(b) TICKET SALES via DFC
    If Ultimate Legends sells tickets through DFC's Stripe-powered
    ticketing:

    Revenue Split:
    - Ultimate Legends receives: 85% of net ticket revenue
    - DFC platform fee: 15%
    - Stripe processing fees deducted before split

(c) SPONSORED PLACEMENT
    Premium feed positioning, banner ads, push notification
    campaigns. Priced per campaign — separate quote on request.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4. WHAT ULTIMATE LEGENDS PROVIDES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

(a) REQUIRED (for free listing):
    - Event information: date, venue, fight card, ticket URL
    - Written confirmation that DFC may list and promote the event
    - This signed agreement

(b) OPTIONAL (for enhanced promotion):
    - Official event poster (high-res, min 1600×2400)
    - Fighter headshots / promo photos
    - Sponsor logos (transparent PNG)
    - Broadcast stream URL or Mux ingest key (for PPV)
    - Model releases for all depicted persons (template provided)

(c) RIGHTS GRANTED:
    Ultimate Legends grants DFC a non-exclusive, worldwide,
    royalty-free licence to:
    - Display event information, fight cards, and results
    - Create derivative promotional materials (thumbnails, banners,
      social crops) from provided materials
    - Feature Ultimate Legends content in DFC's feed and social channels
    - Archive event results and fighter statistics permanently

    Ultimate Legends retains full ownership of all original materials.
    DFC's licence is for promotional and platform display purposes only.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5. CONTENT RIGHTS & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

(a) Ultimate Legends retains all IP rights to their brand name,
    logos, event names, and original content.

(b) DFC retains all IP rights to the DFC platform, technology,
    AI analysis, typographic posters, and original editorial content.

(c) Neither party may use the other's trademarks outside this
    Agreement without written permission.

(d) Ultimate Legends represents that all materials provided do not
    infringe third-party IP rights, and that model releases have
    been obtained for all depicted persons.

(e) If DFC creates promotional materials using provided assets,
    Ultimate Legends will be credited as the source promotion.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6. PAYMENT & STRIPE CONNECT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

(a) For revenue-share services (PPV, tickets), Ultimate Legends
    will onboard to Stripe Connect via DFC's platform.

(b) Payouts are processed automatically:
    - PPV revenue: within 30 days of event, monthly reconciliation
    - Ticket revenue: within 14 days of event
    - Minimum payout threshold: $50 AUD

(c) Full revenue transparency dashboard available in DFC's
    Promoter Portal showing:
    - Gross revenue → Stripe fees → Net → DFC% → DFC cut → Your cut
    - Per-transaction ledger
    - CSV export for your accountant

(d) All financial records retained for 7 years per Australian tax law.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7. TERM & TERMINATION
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

(a) This Agreement is effective from the date of signing and
    continues for 12 months, automatically renewing unless either
    party provides 30 days' written notice.

(b) Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice
    for any reason.

(c) Upon termination:
    - DFC will remove promotional materials within 14 business days
    - Archived event results and fighter statistics remain on DFC
      permanently (public record)
    - Outstanding payments will be settled within 30 days
    - Replay/VOD access for purchased content continues for existing
      buyers until their access expires

(d) Termination does not affect any rights or obligations arising
    from events that occurred during the term.

8. AUSTRALIAN LAW COMPLIANCE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

(a) This Agreement is governed by the laws of Queensland, Australia.

(b) Both parties comply with:
    - Australian Consumer Law (Competition and Consumer Act 2010)

(c) DFC maintains:

9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

(a) DFC is a technology platform. DFC does not organise, sanction,
    or take responsibility for the conduct of combat sports events.

(b) Ultimate Legends is solely responsible for event safety,

(c) Neither party's total liability shall exceed the total fees
