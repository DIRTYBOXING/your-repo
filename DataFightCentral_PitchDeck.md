# Data Fight Central Pitch Deck

---

## 1. Title Slide

**Data Fight Central**  
Empowering Fighters, Coaches, and Fans with Data & Community

---

## 2. The Problem

- Fight sports lack a unified platform for data, training, and social engagement.
- Athletes, coaches, and fans are scattered across tools and communities.

---

## 3. Our Solution

- A modular app for stats, training, device integration, social feed, and marketplace.
- All-in-one platform for the fight community.

---

## 4. Key Features

- **Dashboard:** Real-time stats, performance tracking
- **Plug-In Module:** Universal device support (USB, Bluetooth, Cloud, Custom)
- **Social Feed:** News, posts, mentorship
- **Marketplace:** Gear, services
- **Educational:** Weight cutting, nutrition, NASA science, gym finder
