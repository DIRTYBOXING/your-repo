# UFC Marketing and Technology Playbook

## Overview

A concise summary of how leading MMA organizations combine technology, data, and marketing to convert fan attention into revenue across PPVs, subscriptions, sponsorships, and merchandise.

## Technology Stack

**Digital content and media**

- Platforms: UFC.com, Fight Pass (OTT), YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X, Threads.
- Streaming: DRM protected video delivery for PPV and live Fight Night events.
- Mobile: Native apps with push, SMS, and geolocation for ticketing and localized campaigns.

**Analytics and personalization**

- Social listening and sentiment tools for real-time fan tracking.
- CRM integration for personalized PPV and merchandise offers.
- AI tagging to auto-identify highlight moments and surface clips.

**Infrastructure**

- Cloud CDN with regional edge nodes for low-latency streaming.
- Scalable microservices for content ingestion, encoding, and delivery.
- Data pipelines feeding analytics, CRM, and personalization engines.

## Data Engineering and Automation

- Scheduled aggregation of social metrics and automated leaderboard updates.
- Event-driven triggers that push clips and promos when engagement spikes.
- A/B testing pipelines for thumbnails, CTAs, and email subject lines.

## Marketing Methods

- YouTube: long-form documentaries and episodic series.
- Instagram/TikTok: short highlights and AR filters.
- X/Threads: real-time updates, results, and ticket promos.
- Funnel: Awareness → Engagement → Conversion → Retention.
- Localization: subtitles and region-specific creatives.

## Event and Product Strategy

- PPVs: tentpole revenue events with global campaigns.
- Fight Nights: frequent regional events to maintain habit formation.
- Talent pipelines: Contender Series and regional feeder programs.
- Fight Pass: archive + live niche events + original programming.

## Key Metrics

- PPV buys, subscription conversions, ARPU.
- Engagement metrics: views, watch time, clip virality, social sentiment.
- Funnel metrics: CTR, conversion rate, retention, churn.

## 90 Day Implementation Checklist

1. Instrument social listening and set up real-time alerts.
2. Build a lightweight leaderboard and fan scoring prototype.
3. Automate clip extraction for top moments and schedule cross-platform pushes.
4. Run A/B tests on email subject lines and social CTAs for one event.
5. Integrate transactional email provider and verify domain for receipts and promos.
