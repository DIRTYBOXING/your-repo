## 💡 **Pro Tips for Grant Success**

### Writing Tips

- **Lead with emotion, back with data:** Start with a fighter's story, then show the statistics
- **Use concrete numbers:** "200 crisis interventions" not "significant mental health impact"
- **Show, don't tell:** Include screenshots, demo videos, user testimonials
- **Address sustainability:** How will the project continue after grant funding ends?

### Common Grant Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Vague outcomes:** "Improve fighter wellbeing" → ✅ "Reduce overtraining injuries by 30%"  
❌ **No budget detail:** "Development costs $10K" → ✅ "iOS polish $4K, Android testing $3K, accessibility audit $2K, Firebase hosting $1K"  
❌ **Ignoring competition:** → ✅ "Unlike MyFitnessPal (generic) or TrainHeroic (no mental health focus), we..."  
❌ **Over-promising:** "We'll cure CTE with AI" → ✅ "We'll flag early cognitive decline symptoms and refer to neurologists"

### Follow-Up Strategy

- **Monthly impact updates** to all grant funders (even if not required)
- **Invite grant officers to user testing sessions** (show, don't just report)
- **Publicly thank funders** (social media, app about page)—they love attribution
- **Share success stories** (anonymized) quarterly
