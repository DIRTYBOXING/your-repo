# DataFightCentral Community Guidelines

**Version 1.0 | Last Updated: February 2026**

---

## Our Mission

DataFightCentral exists to protect life, dignity, and wellbeing in combat sports. We support discipline, recovery, respect, and accountability. This platform is a health-first, safety-first environment for fighters, coaches, promoters, and fans.

---

## Core Principles

### We Believe

- **Rest is strength, not weakness**
- **Asking for help is tactical, not shameful**
- **One punch can end multiple lives** (we actively prevent violence outside sport)
- **Silence enables harm**
- **Health includes mind, body, and community**

### Our Platform Is

- ✅ Anti-violence (outside sanctioned sport)
- ✅ Pro-life
- ✅ Pro-recovery
- ✅ Pro-education
- ✅ Pro-support

---

## Prohibited Content & Behavior

### Zero Tolerance Policy

The following are **strictly prohibited** and will result in immediate removal:

#### Violence & Harm

- ❌ Violence glorification or encouragement
- ❌ Street violence, coward punch, or revenge rhetoric
- ❌ Weapons promotion (guns, knives, tactical violence)
- ❌ Self-harm or suicide glorification
- ❌ Threats, intimidation, or bullying

#### Adult & Exploitative Content

- ❌ Pornography, nudity, or sexualized content
- ❌ OnlyFans, escort, or cam site advertising
- ❌ Dating or hookup solicitation
- ❌ Inbox selling or solicitation of any kind
- ❌ Exploitation of any person

#### Substances & Gambling

- ❌ Gambling of any kind (betting, odds, tips, parlays, fantasy wagering)
- ❌ Drug promotion or sales
- ❌ Alcohol advertising
- ❌ Cigarette or tobacco promotion

#### Hate & Discrimination

- ❌ Racism, sexism, or ableism
- ❌ Homophobia or transphobia
- ❌ Religious hatred
- ❌ Class or poverty shaming
- ❌ Any form of hate speech

#### Misinformation & Manipulation

- ❌ Health misinformation or "miracle cure" claims
- ❌ Anti-medical narratives
- ❌ Scams, fake fundraisers, or exploitative sponsorships
- ❌ Pressure selling or predatory financial behavior
- ❌ Dosage advice for supplements or medications

#### Platform Abuse

- ❌ Harassment or intimidation (public or private)
- ❌ Insults, blame, humiliation, or character attacks
- ❌ Shock content, rage-bait, or nonsense posting
- ❌ Political agendas or culture war debates
- ❌ Identity-based arguments or nationality conflicts

## Safety & Crisis Support

### Crisis Language Detection

Our platform monitors for signs of distress. When detected:

1. Immediate support resources are displayed
2. Users receive gentle check-in prompts
3. Helpline information is provided
4. No shaming, mocking, or exploitation of pain

### Youth Protection

- Age declaration required on signup
- Enhanced protections for under-18 users
- No direct messaging between adults and minors
- Youth-specific resources and helplines available

### Reporting System

Every piece of content can be reported for:

- Harassment or abuse
- Unsafe content
- Emotional distress concerns
- Medical misinformation
- Spam or scams

Reports are reviewed by human moderators. We do not publicly shame users.

## AI Coach Guidelines

### What AI Does

- ✅ Interprets health patterns (not diagnoses)
- ✅ Provides educational guidance
- ✅ Offers calm, supportive language
- ✅ Suggests professional help when needed
- ✅ Respects user agency

### What AI Never Does

- ❌ Provides medical diagnosis
- ❌ Replaces human coaches or doctors
- ❌ Encourages pushing through pain
- ❌ Uses aggressive or "grind" language
- ❌ Glorifies violence or harm
- ❌ Shames users for their data

## Alternative Medicine & Recovery

### Permitted Categories

We support educational access to:

- Physiotherapy and sports medicine
- Mental health counseling
- Nutritional guidance
- Sleep clinics
- Recovery and rehabilitation
- Regulated medical cannabis clinics (educational only)
- Complementary therapies (massage, acupuncture, breathwork)

### Required Standards

- All practitioners must be verified
- Clear scope of practice labeling
- "Adjunct support" framing (not replacement care)
- No "miracle cure" claims
- No anti-medical narratives

---

## Monetization Ethics

### How We Operate

- We never exploit pain for profit
- Subscriptions provide clear value
- Advertising is transparent and labeled
- We drive traffic to rightful owners (promoters, PPV, tickets)
- We never hold fight money or PPV revenue directly

### Ethical Advertising

We allow promotion of:

- Gyms and training facilities
- Sports equipment and apparel
- Events and shows
- Recovery services
- Educational programs

We prohibit:

- Alcohol, tobacco, or drug advertising
- Gambling or betting services
- Weapons or violence-related products
- Exploitative financial products

---

## Enforcement

### Violation Consequences

1. **First Offense**: Content removal + warning
2. **Second Offense**: Temporary restriction + education
3. **Third Offense**: Extended suspension + review
4. **Severe Violation**: Immediate permanent ban

### Appeals

Users may appeal enforcement decisions through our support system. Appeals are reviewed by human moderators within 72 hours.
