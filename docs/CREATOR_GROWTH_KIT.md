**What Are FitCredits?**

- Virtual currency redeemable within the DFC ecosystem
- Earned via: referral bonuses, achievement badges, seasonal promotions

**Redemption Paths**:

- **Marketplace**: Buy exclusive training content, coaching calls, merchandise from creators
- **Premium Features**: Unlock advanced analytics, clip scheduling, multi-language subtitles
- **Ad Boost**: Spend FitCredits to promote your top clip (appears higher in trending feed for 24h)
