Purpose: give you the exact contact package for Danny Mac before IBC 4 is confirmed on DFC.

This is the document to use before public promotion, before launch-pack generation, and before any PPV link exists.

---

## 1. Outreach Objective

The goal of first contact is not to sell every feature at once.

The goal is to get Danny Mac to say one of these:

- yes, send me the package
- yes, let’s discuss IBC 4 on DFC
- yes, here are the confirmed event details

That means the first message should be short, concrete, and non-desperate.

---

## 2. What You Are Offering

Present DFC like this:

- a clean PPV event page
- purchase flow
- replay-ready structure
- fighter, gym, and promoter promo packs
- one organized launch system instead of improvised posting

Do not pitch fantasy scale, vague AI language, or unconfirmed outcomes.
