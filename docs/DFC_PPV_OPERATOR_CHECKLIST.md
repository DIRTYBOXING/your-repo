# DFC PPV Operator Checklist

Purpose: give one operator a single checklist for launching one PPV event from approved setup through replay and settlement.

Use this document on a real event. Mark every line before going live.

---

## 1. Event Setup

- event slug is finalized
- event title, date, venue, city, timezone, and promoter name are consistent everywhere
- main event, co-main, and full fight card are confirmed
- event record exists in DFC
- PPV record exists and points to the correct event
- pricing, replay window, and refund policy are entered

---

## 2. Rights And Media Approval

- poster is uploaded through canonical media flow
- banner is uploaded through canonical media flow
- thumbnail assets are uploaded through canonical media flow
- approved media IDs are attached to the event
- no raw unapproved media is still being referenced for launch-critical surfaces
- operator has confirmed that launch, playback, and replay are not blocked by approval state

---

## 3. Launch Pack Production

- hero poster exported in all required sizes
- fight card exported in all required sizes
- countdown assets exported
- captions generated and proofread
- referral links generated for fighters, gyms, creators, and promoter
- payout explainer text included in recipient packs
- sponsor versions exported if sponsors exist

---

## 4. Activation Distribution

- fighter packs sent
- gym packs sent
- creator packs sent
- promoter pack sent
- sponsor pack sent if needed
- all owners have posting windows and escalation contact

---

## 5. Landing Page And Commerce QA

- PPV landing page loads correctly
- poster displays correctly
- fight card displays correctly
- countdown displays correctly
- price displays correctly
- replay policy is visible
- buy-now CTA resolves correctly
- checkout opens correctly from all key entry points

---

## 6. Watch And Broadcast Readiness

- watch route resolves correctly
- access gate behaves correctly for non-buyers and buyers
- playback source is configured
- fallback or partner route is known
- live chat state is acceptable for launch
- operator has incident-response contact list ready

---

## 7. Countdown Execution

- T-14 poster drop sent or scheduled
- T-10 fighter content sent or scheduled
- T-7 gym content sent or scheduled
- T-5 countdown start sent or scheduled
- T-3 hype content sent or scheduled
- T-2 final card content sent or scheduled
- T-24h reminder sent or scheduled
- T-6h reminder sent or scheduled
- T-1h reminder sent or scheduled
- T-10m live-now asset prepared

---

## 8. Go-Live Window

- operator is online and monitoring the event
- playback is confirmed live or failover plan is ready
- live-now blast is sent
- buy link and watch link are verified one final time
- command chat and support contact channel are open
- screenshots or operator notes are being captured for audit

---

## 9. Replay And Highlights

- replay asset is confirmed ready before replay announcement
- replay poster is prepared
- replay caption is prepared
- highlight clips selected and rights-cleared
- T+1 replay post is scheduled or sent
- T+48h highlight pack is scheduled or sent

---

## 10. Settlement And Closeout

- purchases are reconciled
- referral attribution is exported
- promoter totals are reviewed
- fighter, gym, and creator payout basis is recorded
- disputes or failed transactions are logged
- settlement owner signs off
- event retrospective note is captured

---

## 11. No-Go Conditions

Do not launch publicly if any of the following are true:

- approved media is missing
- buy CTA is broken
- watch route is broken
- PPV record and event record do not align
- replay promises are being made without replay readiness
- no operator owns the live window
- no settlement owner is named
