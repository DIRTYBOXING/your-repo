## Problem Statement

Combat sports athletes face unique and underserved challenges:

1. **Mental Health Crisis**: Combat athletes experience 2-3x higher rates of depression, anxiety, and CTE-related cognitive decline than the general population. Yet mental health support is stigmatized and inaccessible.

2. **Unethical Promotion**: Small promoters often exploit fighters with unfair contracts, unsafe conditions, and inadequate medical coverage.

3. **Isolation**: Many fighters train in isolation, lacking access to mentorship, community, and safe recovery pathways.

4. **Data Fragmentation**: Training metrics, health data, and performance insights are scattered across apps with no unified view.

5. **Youth Safety**: Young fighters are particularly vulnerable to exploitation, overtraining, and mental health neglect.

## Our Solution: DataFightCentral

A comprehensive mobile platform (iOS, Android, Web) that combines:

### 1. AI-Powered Health Intelligence

- **AI Coach**: Personalized training guidance that never pushes unsafe limits
- **Redline Health Alerts**: 4-tier risk system (Green/Amber/Orange/Red)
- **Recovery Tracking**: Sleep, heart rate variability, injury monitoring
- **Weight Cut Safety**: Science-based guidance to prevent dangerous cuts

### 2. Community Safety Features

- **FightWire Feed**: Curated opportunities, events, and community updates
- **Mentor Diamond Network**: Verified, vetted mentors on a dark map with neon markers
- **24/7 Crisis Support Access**: Always-free crisis line integration
- **Community Guidelines**: Zero tolerance for exploitation, harassment, gambling

### 3. Recovery Access Hub

- **Medical Cannabis Clinics**: Education-first, regulated pathways only
- **Alternative Therapies**: Breathwork, physiotherapy, sleep clinics
- **Bulk-Billing GPs**: Connecting fighters to affordable healthcare

### 4. Ethical Promotion Tools

- **Promoter Dashboard**: Event creation, fighter database, transparency metrics
- **Fighter Contracts**: Fair-deal guidelines and community ratings
- **Safety Certifications**: Gyms and promoters earn trust badges
