## FAQ FOR DANNY MAC

### Q: How many times should I post the IBC3 promo?

**A:** As many times as you want! Algorithm rewards frequency. Recommended:

- 1 month out: 1x/week
- 1 week out: 1x/day
- Event day: Every few hours

### Q: Will posting the same text get flagged as spam?

**A:** Not if you space it out (wait 12+ hours between identical posts on the same platform). Better strategy: Slightly reword each time or add a new image.

### Q: What if I don't have TikTok/Instagram/etc?

**A:** Focus on the platforms you DO use. Facebook + WhatsApp alone can reach thousands. Quality over quantity.

### Q: How do I know if it's working?

**A:** Check your Facebook/Instagram Insights:

- **Reach:** How many people saw the post
- **Engagement:** How many liked/commented/shared
- **Growth:** Are you gaining followers?

If reach is low in first hour, post again with a different image or at a different time.

### Q: Can I edit the promo text?

**A:** Absolutely! The copy button gives you a template. Feel free to:

- Add your own hype language
- Include sponsor shoutouts
- Customize for specific platforms
- Translate for international audiences

### Q: What's the "FORCE PUMP" button?

**A:** That's in the DFC admin panel (Content Command Center). It's for the tech team to manually trigger a content burst if engagement spikes. You don't need to touch it — the system auto-detects and fires when needed.
