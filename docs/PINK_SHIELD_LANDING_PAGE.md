# 🛡️ Pink Shield Campaign — Protecting Children & Survivors of Violence

## A Shield for Those Who Need It Most

---

## The Reality

### Child Exposure to Violence in Australia

- **40% of children** report exposure to domestic and family violence (FDV)
- Exposure to FDV is the **most common reason** families seek homelessness services
- Many children experiencing homelessness have also experienced violence in the home
- Children exposed to violence face disrupted schooling, mental health challenges, and trauma

### The Impact

Domestic violence doesn't just harm adults — it devastates children:

- **Trauma**: Witnessing or experiencing violence leaves lasting scars
- **Homelessness**: Families fleeing violence often lose stable housing
- **School Disruption**: Children struggle to focus, attend, or succeed academically
- **Mental Health**: Anxiety, depression, PTSD are common outcomes
- **Cycle of Violence**: Without intervention, trauma can perpetuate across generations

---

## What is Pink Shield?

**Pink Shield** is a trauma recovery and violence prevention campaign by the DFC Foundation and **NightChill** partnership.

We provide:

### 🛡️ Immediate Protection

- Emergency shelter support for families fleeing violence
- Safe space coordination with partner organizations
- 24/7 crisis hotline access (Australia & New Zealand)
- Legal support referrals and family safety planning

### 🌙 Trauma Recovery

- Mental health counseling for children and survivors
- Sobriety support for those battling addiction linked to trauma
- Peer support groups facilitated by trained mentors
- Art, music, and movement therapy programs

### 💪 Long-Term Healing

- Community reintegration and stability planning
- Martial arts training for empowerment and self-defense
- Job training and financial literacy for independence
- Parenting support for healing families

## The NightChill Partnership

**NightChill** specializes in trauma recovery and sobriety support, partnering with DFC to provide:

- **Safe Online Community**: Moderated spaces for survivors to connect
- **Sobriety Milestones**: Celebrating recovery journeys without judgment
- **Anonymous Support**: Option to share stories and seek help privately
- **Professional Resources**: Vetted therapists, counselors, and crisis workers

Together, **DFC + NightChill** create a **safe ecosystem** where survivors can heal, rebuild, and thrive.

## Why Combat Sports?

The DFC Foundation leverages the combat sports community because:

### 🥊 Fighters Understand Resilience

Many fighters have overcome personal trauma, addiction, and hardship. They become powerful mentors.

### 🛡️ Gyms Are Safe Havens

Martial arts gyms provide structure, discipline, and a sense of belonging — critical for healing.

### 💪 Empowerment Through Training

Learning self-defense restores agency and confidence to survivors.

### 🤝 Community Protects Community

The DFC network of fighters, fans, gyms, and promoters rallies to protect the vulnerable.
