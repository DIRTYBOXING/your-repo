 1. DEFINITIONS
============================================================

"DFC"        — Data Fight Central, ABN [INSERT ABN],
               operated by [YOUR LEGAL NAME]
"Promoter"   — The event promoter using DFC platform to sell PPV
"Event"      — A live combat sports event streamed via DFC
"MOR"        — Merchant of Record (the party liable for charges)
"Viewer"     — End customer who purchases a ticket
"Platform"   — DFC website, apps, and streaming infrastructure

============================================================
 2. MERCHANT OF RECORD (MOR)
============================================================

2.1. The Promoter acknowledges and agrees that they are the
     Merchant of Record for all transactions related to their
     Events on the Platform.

2.2. The Promoter's Stripe Connected Account (Standard) will
     be the receiving account for all charges. DFC collects
     its commission via application_fee_amount.

2.3. The Promoter is solely responsible for:
     (a) Customer service and refund decisions
     (b) Chargebacks and dispute resolution
     (c) Compliance with applicable consumer protection laws
     (d) Tax collection and remittance in all applicable jurisdictions

2.4. DFC may assist with chargeback evidence (playback logs,
     watermark data) but bears no liability for dispute outcomes.

============================================================
 3. KYC / IDENTITY VERIFICATION
============================================================

3.1. The Promoter must complete Stripe Connect onboarding,
     which includes identity verification (KYC/AML).

3.2. The Promoter must provide:
     (a) Legal name and business registration (if applicable)
     (b) Government-issued photo ID
     (c) Tax identification number (SSN/EIN or equivalent)
     (d) Business address and contact information
     (e) Banking information for payout

3.3. DFC reserves the right to require additional verification
     for promoters with high transaction volumes or flagged risk.

3.4. Promoter represents that all information provided is true
     and current, and agrees to update DFC promptly upon change.

============================================================
 4. IP WARRANTY & CONTENT RIGHTS
============================================================

4.1. The Promoter warrants that they hold all necessary rights,
     licenses, and permissions to stream the Event content.

4.2. The Promoter grants DFC a non-exclusive, worldwide license
     to distribute the Event stream via the Platform for the
     duration of the ticketed viewing window.

4.3. The Promoter agrees not to upload, stream, or distribute
     any content that:
     (a) Infringes third-party intellectual property rights
     (b) Contains unlicensed music, commentary, or footage
     (c) Violates any applicable laws or regulations

4.4. The Promoter shall indemnify and hold harmless DFC from
     any claims arising from IP infringement in Event content.

============================================================
 5. INDEMNIFICATION
============================================================

5.1. The Promoter shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless
     DFC, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from
     and against any and all claims, damages, losses, costs,
     and expenses (including reasonable attorney's fees) arising
     from or related to:
     (a) The Promoter's breach of this Agreement
     (b) Any claims by Viewers related to the Event
     (c) Any IP infringement claims
     (d) Chargebacks, fraud, or payment disputes
     (e) Any injury, damage, or harm related to the Event
     (f) Violations of applicable laws or regulations

============================================================
 6. REVENUE SPLIT & COMMISSION
============================================================

6.1. DFC commission is collected via Stripe's application_fee_amount
     at the time of each charge. The split is as follows:

     | Party     | Share                              |
     |-----------|------------------------------------|
     | Promoter  | Ticket price minus DFC commission   |
     | DFC       | [X]% commission (application fee)   |
     | Stripe    | Standard processing fees            |

6.2. The exact commission percentage shall be agreed in writing
     in a Schedule attached to this Agreement.

6.3. DFC may offer promotional reduced commission rates for
     early partners, subject to separate written agreement.

6.4. Commission is non-refundable once earned, except where
     required by law or in cases of DFC platform failure.

============================================================
 7. REFUND POLICY
============================================================

7.1. As MOR, the Promoter sets refund policy for their Events
     and must clearly communicate it to Viewers at purchase.

7.2. Minimum refund standards (required by DFC):
     (a) Full refund if event cancelled by Promoter
     (b) Full refund if stream fails due to DFC infrastructure
     (c) Proportional refund for significant stream interruptions
     (d) Refund window of at least 24 hours post-event for
         documented stream quality issues

7.3. DFC may process refunds on Promoter's behalf if:
     (a) Promoter fails to respond to refund requests within 48 hours
     (b) Chargeback risk is imminent
     (c) Required by law or payment processor

============================================================
 8. CODE OF CONDUCT
============================================================

8.1. The Promoter agrees to:
     (a) Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
     (b) Ensure fighter safety, proper medical staff, and
         compliance with athletic commission requirements
     (c) Not promote hate speech, discrimination, or violence
         outside of sanctioned competition
     (d) Maintain professional communication with DFC staff
     (e) Not engage in fraudulent, deceptive, or misleading
         practices

8.2. DFC reserves the right to remove any Event or suspend
     any Promoter account that violates this Code of Conduct.

============================================================
 9. TERMINATION & RESERVES
============================================================

9.1. Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days
     written notice.

9.2. DFC may immediately suspend or terminate Promoter access
     if:
     (a) Promoter materially breaches this Agreement
     (b) Promoter's Stripe account is suspended or terminated
     (c) Fraud or illegal activity is detected
     (d) Chargeback rate exceeds 1% of transactions

9.3. Upon termination:
     (a) Promoter's events are removed from the Platform
     (b) Pending payouts for completed events are released
         per Stripe's standard schedule
     (c) DFC may hold reserves for 90 days to cover potential
         chargebacks and refund claims

9.4. Sections 4, 5, 9.3, and 10 survive termination.

============================================================
 10. GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTES
============================================================

10.1. This Agreement is governed by the laws of [JURISDICTION].

10.2. Any disputes shall first be addressed through good-faith
      negotiation for 30 days.

10.3. If negotiation fails, disputes shall be resolved through
      binding arbitration under [ARBITRATION BODY] rules.

10.4. The prevailing party in any dispute shall be entitled to
      recover reasonable attorney's fees and costs.

============================================================

      agreement between the parties regarding its subject matter.

11.2. Amendments: Must be in writing and signed by both parties.

11.3. Force Majeure: Neither party is liable for delays or

11.4. Assignment: Promoter may not assign this Agreement without

11.5. Notices: Must be in writing and delivered to the addresses
      on file with DFC.
