# Data Fight Central (DFC)
### Turning adrenaline into direction. Turning pain into purpose.

DFC is a high‑performance, multi‑module Flutter + Firebase platform designed to protect fighters, guide at‑risk youth, and prevent harm through PPV events, mentorship, community, and real‑time safety intelligence.

This is not a normal social platform.  
It is a guidance and intervention system built from lived experience — created to reach people who never get found.

---

## ⭐ Mission

Use the energy of combat sports to reach people who feel lost — especially men at risk of suicide, women in violent relationships, and youth in toxic environments — and guide them toward mentorship, safety, community, and transformation.

---

## ⭐ Vision

A world where:

- no child grows up without guidance  
- no fighter stands alone  
- no life is lost to violence, addiction, or hopelessness  

DFC turns adrenaline into direction and pain into purpose.

---

## ⭐ Core System

### PPV Gateway
Combat‑sports events create adrenaline and attention. DFC uses this as a gateway to intercept people drifting toward harm.

### Social + Mentorship
Safe, guided spaces to connect with mentors, idols, fighters, and community leaders. Structured support, not toxic feeds.

### Safety + Data
AI‑driven protection: risk detection, injury prediction, event safety scoring, telemetry fusion, and early warning signals.

### Transformation
Pathways toward mental health, discipline, purpose, community, and opportunity.

---

## ⭐ Tech Stack

- Flutter (multi‑platform app)  
- Firebase / Firestore  
- Google Cloud (AI + infra)  
- Mux (PPV streaming)  
- Node/Express microservices  
- Radar ingestion + fusion engine  
- CI/CD pipelines
