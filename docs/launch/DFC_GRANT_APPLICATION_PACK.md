# DFC Grant Application Pack

## Target programs

- Google startup and cloud support pathways
- NVIDIA startup and AI acceleration pathways
- Meta ecosystem and creator-growth support pathways
- Microsoft startup and enterprise enablement pathways

## Core narrative

Data Fight Central (DFC) is a combat sports platform that combines social-first discovery, creator-aligned distribution, and sponsor-accountable monetization with governance-backed engineering operations.

DFC addresses ecosystem fragmentation by integrating fan engagement, creator opportunity, and measurable partner outcomes into one platform.

## What DFC already has in place

- Multi-module product architecture
- CI and quality-gated engineering workflow
- Contributor and governance controls
- Sponsor-facing commercial structure
- Public repository for transparent collaboration

## Value proposition by stakeholder

### Fans

- Better context and less noise
- Higher-quality event-week experiences

### Creators

- Improved distribution and campaign pathways
- Better alignment between audience growth and monetization

### Sponsors

- High-intent combat audience access
- Transparent reporting and activation discipline

## Program-specific framing

### Google

- Cloud-native growth platform with measurable impact and operational maturity

### NVIDIA

- Applied AI use cases: ranking, recommendation quality, moderation assist, campaign optimization

### Meta

- Creator-commerce and social-native distribution in a high-engagement vertical

### Microsoft

- Governance-aware, enterprise-ready platform with B2B sponsor pathways

## Required submission artifacts

- Company one-pager
- Product architecture summary
- 12-month roadmap with milestones
- KPI baseline and target table
- Security and governance summary
- Budget-to-outcome use-of-funds sheet
- Team bios and execution evidence

## KPI set (12-month)

- Monthly active users
- Weekly active creators
- Sponsor retention
- Campaign CTR and attributed outcomes
- Release success rate
- MTTR / operational recovery performance

## Recommended positioning language

DFC is a mission-led, operationally disciplined platform that converts fragmented combat audience attention into measurable value for fans, creators, and sponsors while enabling transparent collaboration through a public codebase.
