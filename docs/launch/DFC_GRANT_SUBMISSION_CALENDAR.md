# DFC Grant Submission Calendar

> Baseline calendar starting from 2026-07-07.  
> Program windows can change; validate each portal before submission.

## Objective

Create a reliable submission rhythm for Google, NVIDIA, Meta, and Microsoft pathways with reusable evidence assets and deadline discipline.

## Grant operating model

- **Monthly cycle:** pipeline review, submission prep, evidence updates
- **Weekly cadence:** owner check-in, artifact completion, risk tracking
- **Single source of truth:** `docs/launch/` + evidence folder links

## Roles

- Submission owner: Founder / grants lead
- Technical reviewer: engineering lead
- Narrative reviewer: partnerships/marketing lead
- Compliance reviewer: operations lead

## 16-week execution calendar

### Week 1 (2026-07-07 to 2026-07-13)
- Finalize master narrative and value proposition
- Freeze KPI baseline table
- Confirm submission owners per program

### Week 2
- Prepare Google-targeted variant
- Prepare Microsoft-targeted variant
- Draft budget-to-outcome mapping

### Week 3
- Prepare NVIDIA-targeted variant (AI workload framing)
- Prepare Meta-targeted variant (creator ecosystem framing)
- Internal review round 1

### Week 4
- Finalize evidence pack v1
- Submission readiness checklist review
- Submit earliest open window (if available)

### Week 5–8
- Program-specific refinements from feedback
- Submit remaining open windows
- Log reviewer questions and response bank

### Week 9–12
- Follow-up sequence for submitted applications
- Refresh KPI progress and execution evidence
- Prepare second-wave submissions

### Week 13–16
- QBR-style grant performance review
- Update narratives with new outcomes
- Lock next-quarter submission calendar

## Monthly milestones

### July 2026

- Next-quarter target list confirmed

## Submission checklist (per program)

- Problem statement (clear, specific)
- DFC solution summary (technical + social impact)
- 12-month roadmap and milestones
- KPI baseline + target table
- Budget use-of-funds mapping
- Governance and reliability summary
- Team and execution credentials

## Evidence room checklist

- Sponsor-facing materials (deck + matrix)
- Public launch narrative
- Architecture and governance docs
- CI/quality gate proof snapshots
- KPI trend updates

## Risk controls

- No last-week narrative rewrites
- No submission without owner signoff
- No stale KPI tables older than 30 days
- Portal deadline checked 48h and 24h pre-submit

## Tracking sheet fields

- Program
- Application URL
- Deadline
- Owner
- Status (Draft / Review / Submitted / Follow-up)
- Last updated
- Next action date

## Recommended cadence meeting (30 minutes weekly)

1. Deadlines and blockers
2. Artifact readiness score
3. Submission decisions for the week
4. Follow-up actions on prior submissions
