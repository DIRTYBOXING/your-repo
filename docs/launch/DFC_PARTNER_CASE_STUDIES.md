# DFC Partner Case Studies (Awareness, Engagement, Conversion)

These case-study templates are designed for sponsor calls, grant evidence, and partnership reviews. Replace placeholders with campaign-specific values.

## Case Study 1 — Awareness Lift (Brand Visibility)

**Partner Type:** Consumer brand / event-week sponsor  
**Objective:** Increase qualified awareness in combat audience windows

### Activation
- Event-week homepage + module placement
- 2 creator co-posts with trackable links
- Branded callouts in relevant DFC surfaces

### KPI targets
- Qualified impressions
- Unique reach
- Share-of-voice in event window

### Outcome summary template
- Impressions delivered: **[X]**
- Unique reach: **[X]**
- Awareness lift vs baseline: **[X%]**
- Key learning: **[What creative/placement worked best]**

## Case Study 2 — Engagement Lift (Audience Interaction)

**Partner Type:** Media, apparel, or performance brand  
**Objective:** Improve action quality on sponsored assets

### Activation
- Sponsored content modules across event week
- Creator amplification with CTA variants
- Mid-campaign optimization (headline, thumbnail, CTA)

### KPI targets
- CTR
- Watch time / session depth
- Save/share/comment rates

### Outcome summary template
- CTR: **[X%]**
- Watch time/session depth: **[X]**
- Engagement lift vs baseline: **[X%]**
- Key learning: **[Which variant drove quality engagement]**

## Case Study 3 — Conversion Lift (Commercial Outcomes)

**Partner Type:** Commerce or subscription-focused partner  
**Objective:** Convert high-intent event traffic into attributable outcomes

### Activation
- Dedicated landing-page integration
- Creator-led offer amplification
- Retargeted follow-up sequence during post-event window

### KPI targets
- Attributed visits
- Conversion rate
- Incremental revenue / CPA improvement

### Outcome summary template
- Attributed visits: **[X]**
- Conversion rate: **[X%]**
- Incremental revenue or CPA delta: **[X]**
- Key learning: **[What improved conversion efficiency]**

## Standard reporting block (attach to every case)

- Campaign window
- Deliverables shipped
- KPI targets vs actuals
- Optimization actions taken
- Partner quote (optional)
- Renewal/expansion recommendation
