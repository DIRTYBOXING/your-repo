# DFC Partner Onboarding

## Purpose

This guide helps new DFC partners launch fast with clear scope, owners, assets, and measurable outcomes.

## Who this is for

- Sponsors and brand partners
- Promotions and event operators
- Community and youth-impact organizations
- Technical integrations and distribution partners

## What partners get

- Co-branded activation support for agreed campaigns
- Distribution through DFC social and event surfaces
- Access to campaign reporting cadence and KPI snapshots
- Governance-aligned execution with approval checkpoints

## Onboarding timeline

### Week 0: Intake + Alignment

- Confirm partner goals (awareness, engagement, conversion)
- Confirm audience, geography, and campaign window
- Assign points of contact and escalation paths

### Week 1: Asset Collection

- Logos, brand kit, approved messaging, legal constraints
- Campaign offers, promo codes, and link destinations
- Creative references and required approval format

### Week 2: Build + QA

- Draft content, placements, and launch sequence
- Internal QA and governance checks
- Partner review and final sign-off

### Week 3+: Launch + Reporting

- Go-live on approved channels and event windows
- Mid-cycle optimization check-in
- Final report and renewal recommendation

## Required assets checklist

- Primary brand logo (SVG/PNG)
- Short boilerplate (50-120 words)
- Offer/CTA language
- Destination URLs with UTM expectations
- Contact owner for approvals (name + email)
- Legal/compliance exclusions

## Success metrics framework

Track outcomes against one primary objective and two supporting metrics.

- Awareness: reach, impressions, unique viewers
- Engagement: CTR, watch time, saves, shares
- Conversion: signups, purchases, qualified leads

## Governance and approvals

- All external-facing assets require partner sign-off before launch
- High-risk claims or sensitive content require additional review
- Any scope changes are logged with owner/date/reason

## Communication cadence

- Weekly async update (email + KPI snapshot)
- Mid-cycle live review for optimization decisions
- End-of-cycle report with recommendations
