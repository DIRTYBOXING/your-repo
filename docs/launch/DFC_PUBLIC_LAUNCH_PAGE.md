# DFC Public Launch Page

## Data Fight Central

**The combat sports operating system for fans, creators, and sponsors.**

Data Fight Central (DFC) unifies fight discovery, creator distribution, and premium monetization pathways into one platform. We turn fragmented combat attention into high-trust community participation and measurable outcomes.

## Why DFC exists

Combat fans currently jump across multiple apps for schedules, context, clips, and purchase paths. DFC brings these into one guided experience:

- Discover: social-first fight content and creator signals
- Understand: event context, storylines, and high-signal updates
- Act: clear sponsor and commerce pathways with transparent reporting

## Who DFC serves

### Fans

- Faster access to meaningful fight content
- Less noise, more relevant context
- Better event-week experience

### Creators

- Improved discoverability in a combat-specific ecosystem
- Better monetization alignment with audience intent
- Platform support for campaign and promotional workflows

### Sponsors

- Concentrated combat audience around high-intent event windows
- Brand-safe placements with governance controls
- Performance reporting mapped to measurable goals

## Platform trust and operations

DFC is built with operational discipline:

- CI-governed release flow
- Quality gate enforcement
- Branch protection and review controls
- Documented contributor and governance policies

Reliability is treated as a product feature, not an afterthought.

## Sponsor and partner pathways

- Sponsor tier matrix with clear value and measurement
- Campaign activation support
- Structured reporting cadence
- Optional pilot-based commercial entry model
