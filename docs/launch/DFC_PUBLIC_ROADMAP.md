# DFC Public Roadmap (2026-2027)

This roadmap communicates execution intent to sponsors, contributors, and community stakeholders. Dates are directional and updated as delivery progresses.

## North Star

Build the most trusted combat-sports platform that turns attention into guidance, safety, and measurable opportunity.

## Pillar A — Product Reliability

### Q3 2026

- Stabilize core routing and shell experience
- Reduce analyzer/test debt on high-traffic modules
- Improve incident playbooks and release hygiene

### Q4 2026

- Harden PPV purchase and entitlement flows
- Expand observability and error triage speed
- Improve mobile/web performance and startup consistency

## Pillar B — Creator + Community Growth

### Q3 2026

- Improve Link-in-Bio conversion surfaces
- Expand creator campaign toolkits and reporting
- Launch structured contributor quickstart pathways

### Q4 2026

- Partner pilot activations with measurable outcomes
- Add case-study publishing cadence
- Improve retention journeys for fans and creators

## Pillar C — Sponsorship + Grants

### Q3 2026

- Execute outreach sequences using sponsor pack
- Submit priority grants based on calendar windows
- Build evidence room artifacts for reviewer confidence

### Q4 2026

- Publish sponsor outcomes summary and renewal offers
- Expand partner onboarding playbook
- Standardize campaign KPI reporting templates

## Pillar D — Governance + Trust

### Q3-Q4 2026

- Maintain branch protection and PR quality standards
- Keep policy/checklist docs current and actionable
- Strengthen contributor and operator runbooks
