## Slide 2 — The Problem

Combat audience value is fragmented across disconnected channels:

- Fans bounce across feeds, clips, event pages, and checkout surfaces
- Sponsors buy impressions but struggle to connect spend to outcomes
- Creators generate demand but lack structured monetization pathways

**Result:** high attention, low accountability.

## Slide 3 — The DFC Solution

DFC unifies the full event-week value chain:

1. **Discover** — social-first combat content and creator signals

3. **Convert** — sponsor activations and commerce/PPV pathways  
4. **Report** — transparent campaign outcomes and optimization insights
