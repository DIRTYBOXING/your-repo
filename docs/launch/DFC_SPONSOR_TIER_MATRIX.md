# DFC Sponsor Tier Matrix

## Commercial framework

This matrix is designed for sponsor clarity, measurable outcomes, and event-week execution discipline.

| Tier | Annual Investment | Best For | Included Value | Measurement |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Prospect Partner | $25,000 | Emerging brands validating combat audience fit | Weekly placement rotation, 1 campaign/month, newsletter inclusion, social support | Monthly report: reach, CTR, engagement, attributed visits |
| Contender Partner | $75,000 | Growth brands requiring repeat event-week visibility | Prospect benefits + event-week feature slots, creator co-post support, campaign planning | Bi-weekly report + quarterly strategic review |
| Champion Partner | $180,000 | National brands running scaled activations | Contender benefits + premium placements, co-branded series, dedicated partner manager | Weekly event-window dashboard + incrementality review |
| Legacy Founding Partner | $400,000 | Category leaders seeking flagship presence | Optional exclusivity, priority inventory, executive steering, custom integrations | Executive dashboard + QBR reporting |

## Add-ons

- Category exclusivity: +$60,000
- Creator amplification pack: +$30,000
- PPV conversion sprint (8 weeks): +$45,000
- Live event war-room reporting: +$20,000/event

## Recommended terms

- 50% upfront / 50% net-30 at launch phase completion
- Brand safety and governance checkpoint before campaign release
- Defined makegood policy for missed guaranteed inventory
- Asset usage rights documented per campaign agreement

## Delivery principles

- Outcome-first reporting over vanity metrics
- Event-window performance analysis
- Sponsor-safe placement governance
- Continuous creative optimization
