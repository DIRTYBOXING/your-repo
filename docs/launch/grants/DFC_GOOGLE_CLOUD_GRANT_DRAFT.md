# DFC Grant Draft — Google Cloud

## Program fit summary
DFC is a cloud-native combat sports platform designed to convert fragmented audience attention into measurable outcomes for fans, creators, and sponsors.

Google Cloud fit areas:
- scalable cloud-native backend operations
- analytics-driven optimization
- measurable social/community impact via responsible platform design

## Problem statement
Combat communities face fragmented experiences across discovery, event context, and conversion pathways. This fragmentation reduces trust, weakens creator opportunity, and limits measurable sponsor value.

## DFC solution
DFC unifies discovery, event context, creator amplification, and conversion into one governed operating system, with transparent KPI reporting and operational quality controls.

## Requested support
- Cloud credits for analytics, storage, and workload scaling
- Architecture review and optimization guidance
- Program support for milestone-driven execution

## 12-month outcomes
- improve active user retention in event windows
- increase creator campaign participation
- improve sponsor outcome visibility (CTR, attributed visits, conversion)
- maintain release quality and operational reliability

## Evidence attached
- Public launch page
- Sponsor deck and matrix
- Public roadmap
- Evidence room checklist and KPI snapshot

## Budget-to-outcome framing
Funds/credits accelerate data infrastructure, campaign analytics, and reporting reliability tied to defined KPI milestones.

## Closing
DFC aligns with Google Cloud’s startup enablement goals: high-impact product infrastructure, measurable outcomes, and disciplined execution.
