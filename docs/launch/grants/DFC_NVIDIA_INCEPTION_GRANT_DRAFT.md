# DFC Grant Draft — NVIDIA Inception / AI Enablement

## Program fit summary
DFC applies AI to improve relevance, safety, and performance in a combat-sports ecosystem while maintaining governance and operational accountability.

NVIDIA fit areas:
- AI-assisted ranking/recommendation quality
- moderation and safety signal support
- campaign optimization and predictive reporting

## Problem statement
Combat audience ecosystems are high-volume and fast-moving, but often low in context quality and safety controls. This limits trust and reduces measurable platform value.

## DFC solution
DFC combines social discovery, event intelligence, and conversion pathways with AI-assisted optimization and transparent KPI reporting.

## Requested support
- NVIDIA startup/AI program support
- technical guidance for model optimization and inference efficiency
- enablement resources for responsible AI scaling

## 12-month outcomes
- improved content relevance and engagement quality
- faster moderation triage support signals
- improved sponsor campaign optimization and conversion quality
- stronger operational confidence in AI-assisted surfaces

## Evidence attached
- Sponsor outcomes framework
- Partner case studies
- Roadmap and governance artifacts
- KPI snapshot templates

## Budget-to-outcome framing
Support accelerates responsible AI experimentation and production hardening tied to explicit KPI milestones and operational safeguards.

## Closing
DFC is an applied AI platform with a clear commercial pathway and measurable public benefit through safer, more accountable engagement systems.
