# DFC Mobile-First Community Engagement Playbook

## Mission Fit

This playbook translates DFC’s mission into practical outreach and content operations that promote healthy choices, discipline, and community safety across combat sports ecosystems.

## Core Principle

If 99% of the audience is on handheld devices, every campaign must be:
- mobile-first
- short-form compatible
- positive and actionable
- measurable

## Sector Mapping (Sponsor + Community Categories)

1. Combat events and promotions
2. Martial arts gyms and disciplines
3. Athlete health and recovery
4. Healthy food and nutrition partners
5. Training gear and equipment
6. Youth mentorship and education pathways
7. Mental wellbeing and social support services
8. Creator and media distribution partners

## Healthy Social Engagement Model

### Do
- Promote discipline, training, mentorship, and community support
- Use positive calls-to-action (join training, request help, attend events)
- Highlight pathways to healthier routines and meaningful goals

### Avoid
- Fear-based messaging without support pathways
- Sensational framing that amplifies harmful behavior
- Unclear or non-measurable campaign objectives

## Campaign Funnel (Mobile)

1. **Attention:** short-form event/creator content
2. **Trust:** mission-safe messaging + social proof
3. **Action:** pilot CTA, signup CTA, mentorship/gym CTA
4. **Retention:** weekly value loops and follow-up touchpoints

## Suggested Content Pillars

- Fight discipline and structured training
- Recovery and nutrition education
- Community spotlight and mentor stories
- Event-week opportunities and local gym pathways
- Sponsor-supported positive initiatives (coffee, food, access support)

## KPI Framework

- Reach quality
- Engagement quality (CTR/watch depth/saves)
- Positive action rate (signups, program requests, event participation)
- Conversion outcomes (pilot, sponsor, partner actions)

## Weekly Operations Rhythm

- Monday: performance review and content plan
- Wednesday: creator/sponsor sync + optimization
- Friday: community impact summary + next-week priorities

## Governance Checklist

- Brand safety approval complete
- Mission alignment verified
- Support pathways included where sensitive topics appear
- Measurement plan documented before launch

## Outputs

- Sponsor-ready campaign briefs
- Community-safe social scripts
- Mobile-first landing CTA flow
- Weekly dashboard updates
