CONSENT & AGREEMENT

By signing below, the undersigned ("Gym") agrees to the following terms
as a sponsored gym participant in the DataFight Central (DFC) Shields &
Gold Coins program:

1. SPONSORSHIP BENEFITS (SPONSORED GYMS ONLY)
   The Gym acknowledges that Shield badges, extra ad placements, and
   Gold Coins promotional credits are available exclusively to gyms that
   hold an active DFC sponsorship agreement. Non-sponsored gyms may list
   events and fighters on DFC but are not eligible for these benefits.

2. FEATURE CONSENT
   The Gym grants DFC permission to:
   a) Display the Gym's name, logo, bio, and photographs on the DFC
      platform, mobile app, and social media channels.
   b) Feature the Gym in "Gym Spotlight" promotions and marketing
      materials.
   c) Reference the Gym's fighters and events to promote the Shields &
      Gold Coins program.

3. FIGHTER CONTENT
   The Gym confirms that any fighter headshots, training footage, or
   biographical information submitted to DFC has been provided with the
   individual fighter's written consent (model release) and that the Gym
   will supply copies of those releases upon request.

4. ACCURACY
   The Gym confirms that all information provided is accurate and agrees
   to notify DFC promptly of any changes.

5. PROMOTIONAL CREDITS
   Gold Coins credits are non-transferable, have no cash value, and may
   only be used for DFC promotional placements. DFC reserves the right to
   adjust credit allocations with 30 days' written notice.

6. PRIVACY & DATA
   DFC will store the Gym's contact information securely and will not
   sell or share it with third parties outside the Shields & Gold Coins
   program. The Gym may request deletion of its data at any time by
   contacting legal@datafightcentral.com.

7. WITHDRAWAL
   The Gym may withdraw from the program at any time by sending written
   notice to legal@datafightcentral.com. DFC will remove featured content
   within 14 business days of receiving the withdrawal notice.

8. DURATION
   This consent remains in effect for 12 months from the date signed and
   renews automatically unless either party provides 30 days' written
   notice of non-renewal.
