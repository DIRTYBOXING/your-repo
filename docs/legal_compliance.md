# Legal & Compliance

## Policies

- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Refund Policy
- Community Guidelines
- Fighter Safety Policy
- Promoter Agreement
- Sponsorship Agreement

## Compliance

- Age restrictions (18+ for PPV combat content, 13+ for general platform)
- Data retention rules (user data: 3 years inactive, logs: 90 days, financial: 7 years)
- GDPR compliance (right to erasure, data portability, consent management)
- Australian Privacy Act compliance (APP 1–13, notifiable data breach scheme)
- Stripe financial compliance (PCI DSS Level 1 via Stripe, no raw card data stored)
- COPPA compliance (no data collection from under-13 users)
- Combat sport regulatory compliance (medical clearance, weigh-in records, commission reporting)

## Agreements

- Promoter Partnership Agreement (sliding revenue share, Stripe Connect onboarding)
- Fighter Monetization Agreement (PPV split, merchandise rights, image licensing)
- Sponsorship Framework Agreement (brand safety, placement guarantees, reporting)
- Gym Affiliation Agreement (data sharing, fighter referral credits)
- Federation Compliance MOU (ranking data exchange, event sanctioning)

## Data Protection

- Encryption at rest (Firestore native + Cloud KMS)
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3 mandatory)
- Access logging (Cloud Audit Logs retained 400 days)
- Incident response SLA (critical: 1 hour, high: 4 hours, medium: 24 hours)
