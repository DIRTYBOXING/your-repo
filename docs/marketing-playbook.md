# DataFightCentral Marketing Playbook

## Purpose

Operational checklist and content plan for DataFightCentral to replicate high-impact MMA marketing tactics.

## Channels and Content Types

- **YouTube** long-form: Embedded style behind-the-scenes and fight breakdowns.
- **Short form**: Instagram Reels and TikTok highlight clips with subtitles.
- **Live**: Watch parties and local commentary streams.
- **Email**: Transactional receipts, event reminders, and segmented promos.

## Automation and Tools

- Social listening for breakout moments.
- Clip extraction pipeline (FFmpeg + simple tagging).
- CRM segments: hardcore, casual, regional, betting audience.
- Retargeting lists for PPV and presale buyers.

## Measurement

- Weekly dashboard: views, watch time, email CTR, presale conversion.
- Post-event analysis: top clips, best performing channels, retention lift.

## Quick content calendar

- Day -14: Announcement + presale open.
- Day -7: Training feature + fighter interview.
- Day -1: Watch party reminder + device instructions.
- Day +1: Highlights + thank you email + upsell.
