## Technical Specifications

**Duration**: 60 seconds (at 24fps: 1,440 frames total)

**Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter optimal)

**Resolution**: 1920×1080 (can be upscaled to 4K if desired)

**Color Palette**:

- **Primary**: DFC neon red `#FF1744`, neon cyan `#00E5FF`
- **Secondary**: neon green `#00FF00`, dark background `#0A0E27` (near-black)
- **Text**: White `#FFFFFF` (95% opacity for legibility)

**Font**: Modern sans-serif (Montserrat Bold, or similar) for all text overlays

**Music Track**:

- **BPM**: 120
- **Genre**: Electronic/synth-pop (upbeat, modern)
- **Recommended**: Royalty-free tracks from epidemic.com, artlist, or similar
- **Vibe**: Startup energy, forward momentum, confidence

**Transitions**:

- Scene-to-scene: Fade (0.3s) or subtle wipe
- Number counters: Tick up at 10x speed (realistic but snappy)
- Zoom in/out: 0.5s smooth ease-in-out

---

## Director's Notes

**Pacing**: Fast (60 seconds is tight). Keep each scene 5–10 seconds max.

**Visual Style**: Minimalist but energetic. Dark backgrounds with neon accent colors. Avoid clutter.

**Animation**: Smooth, professional-grade. No cheap effects. Use:

- Fade transitions
- Smooth zoom/pan
- Counter animations (number ticks)
- Glowing effects on interactive elements

**Narration** (Optional): No voiceover. Let visuals + text + music tell the story. Music does the heavy lifting.

**Key Moments** (Frame-perfect):

- Frame 5s: Knockout impact (most satisfying moment)
- Frame 16s: Engagement counter ticking (shows speed)
- Frame 33s: Conversion counter hitting 399 (proof of scale)
- Frame 40s: Full dashboard reveal ("aha" moment)
- Frame 55s: Final CTA with logo (memorable brand moment)
