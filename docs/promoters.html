What we require before public PPV sales

  • Signed one-page Pilot Licence granting DFC the right to stream and sell the event.
  • Witnessed Statutory Declaration from an authorised promoter representative confirming rights, with supporting documents attached.
  • Stripe Connect Express onboarding so you receive payouts directly — DFC does not hold promoter funds.
  • Technical test pass — DRM and forensic watermarking verified 72 hours before event.

Download the templates above, get the Stat Dec witnessed by a JP or solicitor, then book a tech call to finalise.

How to sign and submit

  1. Download the Pilot Licence and Statutory Declaration — use your browser's "Print / Save as PDF" button on each page.
  2. Get the Statutory Declaration witnessed by a JP or solicitor (Queensland); scan and email to ops@datafightcentral.com.
  3. Complete Stripe Connect onboarding (Express) — takes 5–10 minutes.
  4. Schedule the 72-hour technical test window with the DFC production team.
  5. Once the licence is signed and the tech test passes, your event goes live on DFC.

What DFC provides

  • Production support — multi-camera encoding and CDN delivery
  • DRM-protected player — Widevine, FairPlay, PlayReady
  • Forensic watermarking — server-side, per-viewer unique marks
  • Paywall & PPV checkout — Stripe-powered, promoter paid directly
  • Daily reporting — sales, views, conversion metrics
  • Anti-piracy enforcement — monitoring, takedowns, geoblocking
  • 30-day VOD — highlights package included in pilot
