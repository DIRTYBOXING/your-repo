## SLIDE 2 — PROBLEM

### Combat sports has a $50B data blind spot

**The problems:**

1. **No objective scoring** — Judges use subjective criteria; robberies are common
2. **No safety data** — Head impacts aren't tracked; fighters don't know their risk
3. **No performance analytics** — Fighters train blind; coaches guess
4. **No real Fight IQ** — Talent is hidden; matchmaking is broken

**Quote:**  
_"Boxing is the only major sport still scored by human opinion"_

---

## SLIDE 3 — SOLUTION

### Wearable sensors + AI = Combat Intelligence

**We built:**

- Glove-clip sensors (punch tracking)
- Head sensors (impact monitoring)
- UWB distance tracking (hit/miss verification)
- ATLAS AI (the brain)

**Result:**  
Real-time, objective, data-driven combat analytics

- Sub-centimeter distance accuracy
- Hit/miss detection
