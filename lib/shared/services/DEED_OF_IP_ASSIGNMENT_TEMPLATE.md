## Background

A. The Assignor has independently developed and is the sole owner of certain intellectual property related to the "Data Fight Central" platform.

B. The Assignee has been incorporated to carry on the business associated with the Data Fight Central platform.

C. The Assignor has agreed to assign all of their rights, title, and interest in and to the Intellectual Property to the Assignee in accordance with the terms of this Deed.

## Operative Provisions

### 1. Definitions

In this Deed, unless the context otherwise requires:

**"Intellectual Property"** means all intellectual property rights, whether registered or unregistered, including but not limited to:

(a) **Source Code:** All code for the Data Fight Central platform, including the Flutter application, backend services (TypeScript, Python, Node.js), and related scripts.

(b) **AI Systems & Algorithms:** All proprietary algorithms, including fight matching, feed ranking, AI swarm coordination, detection/triangulation systems, and the "Samurai Swarm" and "Atlas" engines.

(c) **Hardware & Firmware:** All firmware designs, including for nRF52840 devices using Zephyr RTOS, and associated hardware specifications.

(d) **Data & Databases:** All data models, database schemas, and proprietary data structures.

(e) **Trademarks:** The names and logos for "Data Fight Central", "DFC", "CHUCKYA", "Blackbird", "Samurai Swarm", "Atlas", "DIRTYBOXING", and any other related marks.

(f) **Copyright:** All copyrights in original works of authorship, including software, documentation, designs, and marketing materials.

(g) **Trade Secrets:** All confidential business and technical information, including the trade secrets identified in the `ip_protection_plan.txt`.

(h) **Patents:** All inventions, discoveries, and rights to apply for patents.

(i) **Domain Names:** All registered domain names associated with the project, including `datafightcentral.com`.

### 2. Assignment

For the consideration specified in Clause 3, the Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns to the Assignee, absolutely and free from all encumbrances, all of the Assignor's rights, title, and interest in and to the Intellectual Property throughout the world.

### 3. Consideration

In consideration for the assignment under Clause 2, the Assignee agrees to:

(a) Issue `[Number]` fully paid ordinary shares in the capital of the Assignee to the Assignor; AND/OR

(b) Pay the Assignor the sum of AUD $10.00.

The Assignor acknowledges that this consideration is the full and entire value for the transfer of the Intellectual Property.

### 4. Further Assurance

The Assignor agrees to execute all such documents and do all such things as may be reasonably necessary to give full effect to this Deed and to assist the Assignee in perfecting its title to the Intellectual Property. This includes assisting with the formal transfer of any registered trademarks or patents at the request of the Assignee.

### 5. Warranties

The Assignor warrants to the Assignee that:

(a) The Assignor is the sole legal and beneficial owner of the Intellectual Property.

(b) The Assignor has the full right and authority to enter into this Deed and assign the Intellectual Property.

(c) To the best of the Assignor's knowledge, the Intellectual Property does not infringe the rights of any third party.

(d) The Intellectual Property is free from all mortgages, charges, liens, and other encumbrances.

### 6. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Deed is governed by the laws of `[State, e.g., Queensland]`, Australia. Each party irrevocably submits to the non-exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of that state.
