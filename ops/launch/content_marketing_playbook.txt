================================================================
 1. CONTENT TYPES AND PURPOSE
================================================================

| Content type       | Purpose                                          | Primary CTA              |
|--------------------|--------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| Landing page       | Convert visitors into ticket buyers or signups   | Buy ticket / Join waitlist|
| Athlete profile    | Build credibility and emotional connection       | Follow athlete / Share   |
| Feature story      | Longform narrative to create shareable authority  | Read more / Subscribe    |
| Short social clip  | Viral discovery and creator seeding              | Watch full / Buy ticket  |
| Email newsletter   | Nurture and convert warm leads                   | Buy ticket / RSVP        |

================================================================
 2. EVERY PAGE MUST INCLUDE
================================================================

- Hero statement — one bold sentence that explains the value (who, what, why)
- Proof — match results, verified quotes, photos with signed releases
- Social proof — creator endorsements, short testimonials, view counts
- Clear CTA — ticket, join list, or watch demo; most visible element
- Legal line — short note about rights and endorsements if applicable
- Tracking — Pixel, GA4, UTM parameters for every outbound link

================================================================
 3. LEGAL AND IMAGE PERMISSIONS (GET FIRST)
================================================================

- Athlete release — signed consent for use of name, image, and likeness
- Photographer licence — written permission or assignment from image owner
- Endorsement agreement — required if implying sponsorship or paid partnership
- Minor consent — parent/guardian signature for anyone under 18
- Disclosure — label paid posts clearly with "Paid partnership" or #ad

RULE: Do NOT publish any commercial post with athlete image until
you have BOTH the athlete release AND photographer licence stored
in your vault.

================================================================
 4. SEO BASICS FOR EACH LANDING PAGE
================================================================

- Title tag: include athlete name + event + city
- Meta description: 1-2 lines selling the benefit with CTA
- URL: short, readable, includes event slug
- Schema: event schema for ticketed shows to surface in search

================================================================
 5. CONTENT CALENDAR (per event)
================================================================

Week -4: Athlete profile + teaser clip
Week -3: Feature story about training and backstory
Week -2: Behind-the-scenes WebXR teaser and creator challenge
Week -1: Countdown posts, ticket scarcity messaging
Day 0:   Live stream + highlights + immediate replay CTA

================================================================
 6. REPURPOSE STRATEGY
================================================================

- Turn longform into 3 short clips, 5 social cards, 1 email sequence
- Use WebXR hero demo as a landing page embed and link to pairing flow

================================================================
 7. DISTRIBUTION CHANNELS
================================================================

- Post to Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, regional platforms
- Seed creators with a promo pack and paid boosts for top posts
- Use targeted ads with UTM tags to measure promoter ROI

================================================================
 8. CONVERSION AND ANALYTICS
================================================================

Events to track:
- page view, CTA click, ticket purchase, entitlement issued, playback start

KPIs:
- Conversion rate (landing -> ticket)
- Cost per acquisition
- Email open rate
- Replay watch time

Dashboards:
- GA4 for funnels
- Meta Pixel for ads
- Stripe for payments
- Grafana for backend health

A/B tests:
- Hero headline, CTA color/text, price points, scarcity messaging

================================================================

| Channel                          | Reach         | Cost          | Speed    | Best use                                    |
|----------------------------------|---------------|---------------|----------|---------------------------------------------|
| Social ads (Meta, TikTok, YT)   | Broad/targeted| Medium/scale  | Fast     | Drive ticket sales and landing conversions  |

| Landing pages and blogs          | Organic + paid| Low to medium | Fast     | Capture emails, tell stories, convert       |
| Creator seeding and UGC          | Viral potential| Low to medium | Fast     | Social proof and shareable clips            |

 13. METRICS, BUDGET AND QUICK TESTS
================================================================

Primary metrics:
- Landing page conversion rate
- CPA (cost per acquisition)
- Ticket sales
- Playback starts
- Retention

Initial budget test:
- $300-$1,000 across Meta and TikTok
- Split into 3 ad sets for 3-7 days:
  1. Cold lookalike audience
  2. Retargeting (site visitors)
  3. Creator boosted posts

Creator seeding:
- 5-10 creators: free tickets + $50-$300 depending on reach
- Track promo codes per creator

 15. ONE-PAGE STRATEGY (RUN TONIGHT)
================================================================

   - List 3 athletes: mark each as Permission Ready / Need Photo
     Release / Need Photographer Licence

2. Build single conversion funnel per event
   - Landing page: hero, athlete profile, proof, CTA Buy Ticket, legal line
   - Short story blog linked from landing page for engagement and SEO

3. Seed creators and influencers

   - Offer free tickets + promo fee or revenue share
   - Provide promo pack: 15s clip, 30s clip, 3 captions, poster, UTM links

4. Run a small paid test
   - 3 ad sets: cold lookalike, retargeting, creator boosted
   - Measure CPA to ticket conversion and scale winner

5. Use WebXR as premium hook
   - Embed WebXR teaser on landing page
   - Offer exclusive metaverse preview for early buyers
   - Increases perceived value and social shareability
