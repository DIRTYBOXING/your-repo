STRIPE CONNECT ONBOARDING GUIDE — DATA FIGHT CENTRAL
=====================================================
For promoters receiving PPV payouts through DFC.

OVERVIEW
--------
DFC uses Stripe Connect (Express) so promoter funds route directly to
your bank account. DFC does NOT hold your funds — Stripe splits the
payment at the moment of purchase.

HOW IT WORKS
1. You complete Stripe Express onboarding (5-10 minutes).
2. Stripe verifies your identity (KYC) and bank details.
3. When a fan buys PPV access to your event, Stripe automatically:
   - Sends your share (e.g. 60%) to your connected account
   - Retains DFC's commission (e.g. 40%) as application fee
4. You see payouts in your Stripe dashboard within 2-7 business days.

STEP-BY-STEP FOR PROMOTERS
---------------------------
Step 1: Click the onboarding link DFC sends you by email.
Step 2: Enter your business details:
        - Business name and ABN (Australian promoters)
        - Business address
        - Representative details (name, DOB, ID)
Step 3: Enter your bank account details for payouts.
Step 4: Agree to Stripe's terms of service.
Step 5: Submit — Stripe will verify within 1-2 business days.
Step 6: Confirmation email from Stripe when your account is active.

SANDBOX TESTING (BEFORE LIVE EVENT)
-------------------------------------
- DFC will create a test PaymentIntent using Stripe test mode.
- You will see a test payout appear in your Stripe test dashboard.
- Confirm the amount matches expected split.
- Report any issues to ops@datafightcentral.com.

LIVE PAYMENT FLOW
------------------
1. Fan visits event PPV page on datafightcentral.com.
2. Fan clicks "Buy PPV Access" and enters card details.
3. Stripe creates a PaymentIntent with:
   - transfer_data.destination = [your connected account ID]
   - application_fee_amount = [DFC commission in cents]
4. Stripe charges the fan, routes your share instantly.
5. Stripe holds a short reserve (7-14 days) for chargebacks.
6. Payout arrives in your bank account per your Stripe payout schedule.

REPORTING
---------
- DFC provides daily email reports during event window.
- Final settlement report within 7 days of event.
- Full payout reconciliation within 30 days.
- You can also view all transactions in your Stripe dashboard at
  https://dashboard.stripe.com

CHARGEBACKS AND DISPUTES
--------------------------
- If a buyer disputes a charge, Stripe notifies both DFC and you.
- DFC will respond to disputes using purchase evidence and DRM logs.
- If a chargeback is upheld, the amount is deducted from the reserve.
- DFC documents chargeback handling in the Pilot Licence.

FAQ
---
Q: Does DFC hold my money?
A: No. Stripe routes your share directly to your connected account.
   DFC only receives the agreed commission via application_fee.

Q: What fees does Stripe charge?
A: Standard Stripe processing: ~2.9% + $0.30 per transaction.
   These are deducted before the revenue split calculation.

Q: How long until I get paid?
A: Stripe's standard rolling payout schedule (typically 2-7 business
   days depending on your country and account age).

Q: What if I need to change my bank details?
A: Log into your Stripe dashboard and update under Settings > Payouts.
