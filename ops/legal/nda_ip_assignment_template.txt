------------------------------------------------------------
 1. DEFINITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

"Confidential Information" means all nonpublic technical,
business, financial, creative, and operational information
disclosed by Company to Worker, including but not limited to:
source code, algorithms, designs, business plans, customer
data, pricing, strategies, prototypes, firmware, and trade
secrets.

"Work Product" means all code, designs, models, inventions,
discoveries, improvements, documentation, and deliverables
created by Worker in connection with Company work.

------------------------------------------------------------
 2. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

2.1. Worker will keep all Confidential Information strictly
     confidential and will not disclose it to any third party
     without prior written consent from Company.

2.2. Worker will use Confidential Information only for the
     purpose of performing work for Company.

2.3. Worker will restrict access to Confidential Information
     to those who need to know and who are bound by similar
     confidentiality obligations.

2.4. Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this
     Agreement for five (5) years, or indefinitely for
     information that constitutes trade secrets.

2.5. Upon termination or Company's request, Worker will
     return or destroy all copies of Confidential Information.

------------------------------------------------------------
 3. ASSIGNMENT OF IP AND WORK FOR HIRE
------------------------------------------------------------

3.1. Worker assigns to Company all rights, title, and interest
     in and to all Work Product created during the engagement,
     including all intellectual property rights (copyright,
     patent, trade mark, trade secret, and all other rights).

3.2. Worker agrees that all Work Product is a "work made for
     hire" and Company is the sole owner of all copyrights.
     If any Work Product is determined not to be a work made
     for hire, Worker hereby assigns all rights to Company.

3.3. Worker will execute any documents and take any actions
     necessary to perfect Company's ownership of Work Product.

3.4. Worker will not use Work Product for any purpose other
     than Company work without prior written consent.

------------------------------------------------------------
 4. MORAL RIGHTS
------------------------------------------------------------

4.1. To the maximum extent permitted by law, Worker consents
     to Company's use of Work Product without attribution and
     waives any moral rights in the Work Product.

4.2. Where moral rights cannot be waived by law, Worker agrees
     not to assert them against Company.
