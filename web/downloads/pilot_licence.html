2. Commercial Terms

  • Revenue split: Promoter 60% / DFC 40% of PPV gross receipts after payment processing and platform fees.
  • Alternate link-only split: Promoter 85% / DFC 15% (if promoter supplies clean stream and no production support is required).
  • Payment flow: Promoter to onboard Stripe Connect Express; DFC will create PaymentIntents and route promoter share using transfer_data.destination. DFC will not hold promoter funds except for a short reserve for chargebacks (7–14 days).
  • Settlement: Daily reporting; final settlement within 30 days of event.

3. Technical and Delivery

  • Promoter supplies clean feed (SDI/RTMP/HLS) to DFC ingest endpoint by (minimum 72 hours before event).
  • Backup feed required.
  • Technical test 72 hours before event.
  • Final run-through 24 hours before event.
  • DFC will apply multi-DRM (Widevine/FairPlay/PlayReady) and server-side forensic watermarking.

4. Warranties and Indemnity

  • Promoter warrants it owns or controls the broadcast and distribution rights for the Event and has obtained all necessary fighter releases, music clearances, and sponsor permissions.
  • Promoter indemnifies DFC against third-party claims arising from breach of these warranties.
  • DFC liability limited to fees paid for the event or AUD 50,000, whichever is greater, except for willful misconduct.

5. Termination

  • Material breach not cured within 14 days of written notice allows the non-breaching party to terminate.
  • On termination DFC will cease distribution within 14 business days.
  • Final settlement calculated pro rata; outstanding payments still owed.
  • Surviving clauses: indemnity, confidentiality, dispute resolution.

6. Confidentiality

Neither party will disclose commercial terms without prior written consent, except as required by law.

7. Dispute Resolution

  • Good faith negotiation first (14 days).
  • Mediation second (under ACICA rules).
  • If unresolved, arbitration under Queensland law.
  • Governing law: Queensland, Australia.

8. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
